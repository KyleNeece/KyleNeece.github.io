##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Origins/Adolescence
Alex grew up in the slums of Ravnica, a commoner who became an aspiring actress and as such joined a Ravnica Studio that preforms shows for noble folk. What she didn't know was that the studio was a front for the Seelie Court's intelligence agency. Her formidable acting skills along with her precise rythmic movements and thinking impressed them enough that Mark recruited her. Ever since, she has worked with the Seelie court on many covert operations becoming a skilled disguise women. At some point after the three way war between the Peacekeepers, Seelie Court, and Unseelie Court, Freya was placed in Thieves Metropolis as a undercover agent. 
###### The Felwinter Saga
Freya was one of Queen Titania's main intelligence and black ops agents, along with Mark and Daliah. She was on the same mission as Mark and Daliah when dealing with a criminal underworld league. Freya was also unaffected by the Pamnesia spell due to being under the Seelie Courts service (to Queen Titania).
