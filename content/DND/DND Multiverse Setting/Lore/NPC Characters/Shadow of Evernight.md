The Shadow of Evernight is a [[Great Old One]] originating from the [[City of Evernight]] within the Shadowfell

##### Brief History/Timeline   
The Shadow of Evernight is one of the three Great Old Ones who was the offspring of Dox Vulethix (Outer God Great Old One), and [[Maren, Queen of Evernight]]. The Shadow of Evernight walked among the city for centuries. Though it never achieved the level of power of an Outer God
