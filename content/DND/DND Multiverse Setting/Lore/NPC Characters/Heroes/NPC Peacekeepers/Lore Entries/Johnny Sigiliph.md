##### Personality 
Johnny Sigiliph's character on the outside looks average, as when someone attempts to size him up or analyze him, he seems totally normal. Due to how strong he is, he is very indifferent and goes through most things in life with little to no effort. Although he doesn't show strong emotions to others Johnny genuinely cares about other people and has a good spirit, as when someone close to him gets hurt or dies, he enters a state of tranquil anger and decides to not hold back his immense power.
##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Adolescence
Johnny Sigiliph grew up with his sister, Emily, in a militaristic family. During his youth, he was always considered weak and average, always losing to his sister in sparring and always failing in his training. His parents loved his sister more so then him. He accepted this, as he knew that he had to get stronger in order to be noticed, and so he could defend himself. Everyday from this realization he did 100 pushups, 100 situps, 100 squads, and a 10 kilometer run. Eventually, he became so strong that any physical related activity became easy for him, and he became the strongest in his family. He then set off for Peacekeeper academy as a transfer student, following his sister's footsteps, and wishing to be challenged. 
###### Twin Prodigies Arc
Johnny Sigiliph was one of the main exchange students that came for a semester abroad at [[Peacekeeper Academy]], along with the rest of the party that would become rivals with Jackson Felwinter's crew. Johnny made friends with the Malatar, Lillith, Ryū, Hank, and Ariá during exchange student orientation, and him and his group approached Emily and her friends after an all school assembly that was about introducing the international students. The two groups then became rivals, competing in everything.
###### House Tournament Arc
Johnny, like the rest of Jackson's and his group, finished very high in most of the events. He lost to his sister by disqualification, due to refusing to fight because he didn't want to fight his sister because it would've been a "drag". 
###### The Illithid Mage Arc
During Arangol's first invasion, Johnny was one of the key students that drove off the invasion. A gigantic mutated wendigo was spawned in as a trump card for Arangol to leave. This was the perfect matchup for Johnny, as he essentially beat it all by himself due to no one else meeting the conditions to hurt it. 
###### Betrayal Arc
When Azriel invaded, he targeted Johnny with mages in order to play off is stupidity. He made many illusions of soldiers and kept throwing annoying enemy types at him so that he was stalled and would not interfere with his plans.
###### Declaration of War Arc
Johnny went with his sister Emily back home and called upon her family and their armies in order to protect Sigil, she was one of the main frontliners, who would clear a path for Jackson to get to Azriel
###### Panmnesia Arc
Johnny was fighting the strongest disciple of Tartarus when the Pamnesia spell hit, losing all of his memories of the events
###### Recruitment
Johnny was auto recruited into the peacekeeper force and given S-Rank
