##### Personality 
Nathan Murgul grew up in the underdark, where he was a member of the Seldarine following. He stood out among the other's as most of the others who were worshipers were either Duegar or Drow Elfs. What was unique about Nathan was his Psionic abilities, which tend to be rare throughout the forgotten realms. Nathan set out to become a peacekeeper, to spread the influence of the Seldarine.
