##### Brief Summary 
Jotor Sparklefen is a Gnome Artificer originating from the plane of Eberron and is a S-Rank Peacekeeper

##### Personality 
Jotor is generally a very knowledgeable and crafty gnome who blends technology and magic at very high levels. Jotor is always thinking about his next creation, as well as new innovative ways to tackle problems. With the invention of his steel companion, who he can alter their appearance at will, Jotor found the secret to imbuing machinery with life. While Jotor is normally cool headed and calm, he instantly blows comedic fuses when he is called little and is talked to in a taunting baby voice.
##### Brief History/Timeline  
###### Adolescence
Jotor was born in Ebberon, his love for artifice originated from his huge creativity as a kid. Jotor was a mathematical genius, which is reflected in his ability to understand artifice. His parents loved him dearly as they lived a peaceful life on Ebberon. 

With genius comes trouble however, as when Jotor was a kid he was bullied a lot in school. His dislike for those who bully and hurt others for fun inspired him to become strong through intelligence, using his artifice skills to create the foundations of what would later be his greater works, such as his steel companion
###### Prelude
After fleeing into Ebberron and wandering with no purpose for a few days, mourning the loss of his best friend ______ due to the surprise betrayal of Lady Pain, [[Will Felwinter]] stumbled across Jotor and his steel companion, who was defending himself from a circle of Assassins. While Will during this state would've normally turned his eye away due to his lost will to fight, he noticed that Jotor was fighting to protect, not to kill. 

Seeing this, Will helped Jotor, using the pommel of his broken Blade in order to defeat of the Assassins. The two defeated the Assassins, and Jotor asked what happened to his blade. 

Due to rumors of Lady Pains actions, combined with the broken Bowblade and loss of it's sentience, Jotor instantly recognized the situation with his high intellect. He took Will back to his shop, and devised a plan to reconstruct the blade. While it was impossible to regain it's sentience and speech, Jotor was sure that the emotional aspect of the blade could return. Adventuring the Alara, the two traveled to Alara santuary, in search of a fraction of the Gem of Alara. Arriving at a sacred village, the villagers were in despair as their village resources had gone dry and due to Lady Pain's multiversal scale magic, the growth of such resources has temporarily stopped. Jotor, using his immense intellect and Will Felwinter's physical prowess in labor, was able to restore their land. As a result, the two were gifted a fraction of the Gem of Alara, allowing for Will to recraft his Bowblade.
###### Prelude Cotninued
Jotor helped participate in the final battle versus Lady Pain and her army. Jotor played a key roll in figuring our how to raid the center of Sigil, as well as how to unseal magical being's powers when they were stolen by Lady Pain's main underlings. 
###### The Twin Prodigies Arc
After the events of the [[Epilogue to Cour 1 and Upbringing]] and before the [[2.1_Twin Prodigies Arc]]Twin Prodigies Arc, Jotor created Will's son, Jackson, a special Sunblade with the color blue. During this time as well, through his research several years after the battle for Sigil, Jotor found that new, different types of magic users began to appear across the multiverse. Wondering about the phenomena, Jotor set out on an adventure to explore. Under unfortunate circumstances, Jotor became lost, unable to find his way back to [[Sigil]].
###### Panmnesia Arc
It was only when [[Azriel Darkstar]] transformed into an ascendent being with the Runes of the Multiverse was Jotor able to begin traveling back. By the time he got to the scene, the battle was over, and [[(WORK IN PROGRESS) Jackson Felwinter]] notified him that they were going to begin a Multiversal Panmnesia Memory Wipe ritual. However, before Jotor's memory is wiped, he magically imbues parts of his memory into [[Yasu-2]]. After the ritual, Jotor losses his memory.
###### Recruitment
Jotor was recruited near the end of his adulthood moving into his grandhood. Despite his age, Jotor is still a adequate combatant and has not lost a step. Before the transfer of the Peacekeeper ownership to Jackson Felwinter (Will Felwinter's son), Will recruited Jotor, who is now the lead weapon designer and artifact researcher of the organization.
