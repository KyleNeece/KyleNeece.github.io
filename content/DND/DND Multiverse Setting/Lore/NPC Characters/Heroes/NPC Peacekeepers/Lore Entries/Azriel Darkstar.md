##### Personality 
Azriel was a kind, selfless, intelligent person who always would help others in need. However, despite this he was always secretly depressed, as many people would not accept him or would bully him due to his appearance. Aasimar hated him because of his devilish features, and Tieflings would hate him because of his Aasimar like features. Moreover, humans would hate him or be jealous of him because of his supernatural features and immense power, often referring to him as a Nepo Baby. Azriel was born with the power to read runes, a mutation that occurred in his family bloodline. He studied hard to learn this new power. This power was not only new to him, but generally new to the universe, as after the War on [[Sigil]], many energies were released, both changing the fundamental way magic worked as well as bringing fourth new powers and capabilities for both non-magical people and magical people.
##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Adolescence
When Azriel was just a kid, his parents died at the hands of Lady Pain. They were very good friends with [[Will Felwinter]] and as such Will Felwinter took him into his care. Azriel grew up alongside Jackson, and the two were brothers to each other. When Azriel was old enough to move out, Will made sure that Azriel was well taken care of and watched, providing him the resources he needed to be successful. With the opening of the peacekeeper academy, Azriel was enrolled with Jackson.
###### Twin Prodigies Arc
[[Jackson Felwinter]] and Azriel on the way to the academy intervened in a minor threat, with a few gangs causing trouble. Despite their young age, Azriel and Jackson defeated them effortlessly, after which they went to the academy. Azriel was also considered a prodigy as much as Jackson was, with the problem that others would gossip and look at Azriel like he was some sort of monster. Despite it all, Jackson still continued to hang around him and be his friend, which confused many of the students. During the house selection ceremony, Azriel was the first peacekeeper ever to cause a stall in the selection, as they were unable to put him into a house that would best fit due to being well rounded. As a result, they decided to put him into House Catalyst. 
###### House Tournament Arc
During the House Tournament Arc, everyone was eager to see Azriel's power on display, as close to no one has seen a runekeeper in actual action
Azriel finished very high in the Social Networking challenge as well, essentially indimidating he way through 
In the Gem Hunt Challenge, Azriel easily passed, using his unique magical rune abilities to pinpoint a gem and take it
In the Battle-Madness Challenge, Azriel breezed through each round without breaking a sweat. When he made it to the final four however, things became hairy. 
Azriel faced off against Ryū Hoshi. For the first bit of the battle [[Ryū Hoshi]] had Azriel on the defense, though he was not trying. Ryū ended up winning the fight, as Azriel didn't release his true power in fear for other's safety.
In the final round Jackson and Ryú faced off, a heavily anticipated fight. Both did not hold back, showing immense power. However, the fight was cut short, as Arangol's forces began to invade.
###### The Illithid Mage Arc
When [[Arangol]] showed up, he showed immediate interest in Azriel, seeing his dark power, figuring he would be a perfect candidate to use the power of the multiversal runes to full effect. He immediately targeted Azriel, though made sure to test him rather then actually fight him. Azriel was able to surprise him, making him sweat. However, Arangol wanted to push him further, targeting Alexis to see how he would react. Azriel as predicted saved her from fatal harm, healed her, and became pissed off. He unleashed his full power, and began to go head to head with Arangol along with Jackson, who joined him in the fight. The Illithid Lich realized that if he didn't begin to try he was gonna lose, so he began to use more of his power. Jackson and Azriel were overwhelmed, with Will making it just in time in order to stop and push back Arangol's forces. Before Arangol left, he cast an unknown curse on Azriel. He eventually apologized to Olivia for unleashing his dreadful power, but she accepted it as she understood that it was necessary to use in order for Sigil to be safe. However, she noticed a change in Azriel's mental state. Olivia would ask if Azriel was feeling ok. Azriel, who wanted her to not worry about him continued to say he was ok, until one night, he disappeared from the academy. Olivia asked and asked around Sigil, but not a single soul knew where Azriel had gone to.
###### Betrayal Arc
On Azriel's crusade, [[Tokka]] stood between him and his goal, saying he will not allow for him to pass. Azriel, too immensely powerful at this point for the Tortle made quick work of him, effortlessly defeating and inflicting fatal wounds that would later kill him. Azriel then ran into [[Emily Sigiliph]], who cried tears of sadness as she had to confront her good friend. Blinded by anger, she rushed at Azriel, only to be grievously wounded by his guard. Holding pity, Azriel continued on. He eventually ran into [[Yasu-2]], who he instantly sealed away due to being able to turn into an avatar of his god, Eldath. Azriel then made it to the rune, however, [[Olivia Birdsong]] stood between it, sobbing and asking Azriel why he is doing what he is doing. Azriel tells her that he is going to reconstruct the multiverse, where everyone can live in harmony and no evil can exist. Olivia then makes the point that no one is truly free if they are controlled by fear, as she tells him to stop one last time. Azriel, having totally lost himself, attempts to strike her down to put her out of her psychological misery, only to be blocked and challenged by Will Felwinter, who tells him how he is sorry for not raising him right and teleports Alexis to a safe location. The two clash, causing the world to shake. In a close but long fought battle, Azriel finally strikes down Will, and takes the rune for himself, completing his ascension to a greater being and fully becoming Tartarus. 
###### Declaration of War Arc
Azriel, now Tartarus returns to Arangol, telling him the work is done. Arangol thanks him, and tells him to prepare for the upcoming war as he suspects Sigil to launch a counter attack.
Surely enough, Sigil launches a counter attack, where Tartarus's forces clash with the opposing Sigil and many more plane wide forces. Being the general of his army Tartarus did not engage, until Jackson along with Will Felwinter's old party, came to his throne room to stop him. 
The 6 vs 1 fight ensured, with both sides not letting the other breathe. Tartarus summoned his 3 highest ranking generals in order to make it a 4 v 6, but it was not enough was the opposing force kept pushing him back on the defensive. Tartarus finally was pushed to release the 7 runes full power, but due to not being worthy enough to become a god, he lost control and became an monstrosity. In this state he defeated and killed everyone but Jackson. However, their sacrifices were intentional, as Jackson was able to land the killing blow, defeating Tartarus.
