		- **Condo Description**
			- A heavy oaken door, reinforced with dwarven steel filigree. If enter through the door it swings open to reveal a grand hall illuminated by floating braziers, their flames fueled by unseen enchantments. Rich tapestries depicting ancient legends adorn the stone walls, while an intricate mosaic floor of sapphire and gold tile glows softly in the dim candlelight.
			- The main chamber boasts a high vaulted ceiling supported by carved stone pillars, each depicting a different patron deity of prosperity. Velvet-upholstered divans and hand-carved wooden furniture with gilded accents surround a central fireplace of enchanted amber, which crackles with a heatless golden flame, warming the air but never leaving soot or smoke.
			- The dining hall is equally lavish, with a long, obsidian table inlaid with runes that keep meals warm indefinitely. A stained-glass ceiling allows moonlight to filter through, casting soft colored patterns onto the floor. A stone archway leads to an open-air balcony lined with sculpted gargoyles, overlooking the castle district and its torch-lit avenues.
			- The master suite is a marvel of medieval luxury: a four-poster bed draped in silk and fur rests upon a dais of carved oak, while an enchanted brazier releases a calming lavender mist each evening. A private bath chamber holds a sunken marble tub, its water perpetually warmed by a minor elemental bound to the estate. The walls of this sanctuary are inscribed with protective glyphs, warding against unwanted scrying or intrusion
			-  A private study features a grand desk of dark mahogany, its drawers enchanted to lock against all but the owner’s touch. Floor-to-ceiling bookshelves hold rare tomes bound in dragonhide, while a secret alcove behind a moving bookshelf reveals a hidden chamber—perhaps for private dealings or safekeeping of arcane relics.
