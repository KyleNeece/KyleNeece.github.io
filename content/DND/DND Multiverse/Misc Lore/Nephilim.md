Nephilims are the offspring of a Celestial Entity and a Demonic Entity, as a result, they are usually treated as an abomination. This is because they mainly have the traits of the human, but inherit traits of both Celestial and Demonic. They live up to 150 years and potentially beyond, and their heritage is hinted at by the features they bear. 

###### **Features**

**Ability Score Increase.** You increase one ability score by +2 and another different ability score by +1

**Age.** Nephilim mature at the same rate as humans, but they can live up to 150 years and beyond

**Alignment.** Nephilim's tend to lean to true neutral. A balance of both heritages.

**Size.** Nephilim have the same range of height and weight as humans. Your size is Medium.

**Speed.** Your base walking speed is 30 feet.

**Darkvision.** You can see in dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light, and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can't discern color in darkness, only shades of gray.

**Otherwordly Resistance.** You have resistance to fire damage and radiant damage.

**Touch of Life/Death**. You can harness the inner power of your bloodline to heal or harm. You can use your action to heal or a attack roll you land to roll a number of d4's equal to your proficiency bonus. The creature regains or takes additional damage equal to the total rolled. Once you use this trait, you can't use it again until you finish a long rest. 

**Languages.** You can speak, read, and write Common and Celestial.
