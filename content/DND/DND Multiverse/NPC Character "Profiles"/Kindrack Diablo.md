##### Brief Summary 
Kindrack Diablo is a military veteran of the Unseelie court and rules of the military self governed Thieves Metropolis as a Kingpin

##### Personality 
Kindrack Diablo is a militaristic leader and dictator. Some see him as a terrorist or gangster, others see him as a messiah or a leader of paradise away from government law influence and restrictive living. He is very physically capable and uses biomagical implants to help with using and defending against magic. Despite his militaristic background, Diablo also has very high battle intellect and political charisma. 
##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Origins/Adolescence
Originally a part of the Zhentarim as a kid, Kindrack had a knack for fighting and thieving. He eventually sought better use for his talents and made his way to the Unseelie court, to enlist in their military force. He led a squadron of soldiers, and was a renowned leader. After the three way war between the Peacekeepers, Seelie and Unseelie courts, he abandoned his affiliation with the court and started his own metropolis and state/country, along with other of his loyal followers during his time as a platoon leader . 
###### The Felwinter Saga
Kindrack was one of the Queen of Air and Darkness's most decorated soldiers during his service to the court. After he abandoned his duty, he used his experience in complicated operations, organization and leadership skills, harsh punishment techniques and political knowledge to create and start his own domain, which is backed by his loyal soldiers and lots of military personal. Its a profitable lawless black market money scheme outside of noble and corporate influence. The metropolis became a place for those who want to disappear or live outside of lawful ruling, but at the price of the ease of falling into poverty.
