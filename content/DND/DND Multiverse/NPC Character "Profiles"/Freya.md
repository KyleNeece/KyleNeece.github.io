##### Brief Summary 
Freya is a half quickstep originating from Ravnica and a agent of the Seelie Court
##### Appearance 
![[freya.png]]
##### Personality 
Freya is duty focused, charismatic, laid-back, talkative, yet cold at the same time. She shares a same sense of loyalty and duty as Mark, but not enough to the point where she is blinded by it. Similar to Mark she has seen her fair share of battle and is hardened by such events. She still enjoys acting like she originally dreamt of pursuing as a career. 
##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Origins/Adolescence
Freya grew up in the slums of Ravnica, a commoner who became an aspiring actress and as such joined a Ravnica Studio that preforms shows for noble folk. What she didn't know was that the studio was a front for the Seelie Court's intelligence agency. Her formidable acting skills along with her precise rythmic movements and thinking impressed them enough that [[Mark Grayson]] recruited her. Ever since, she has worked with the Seelie court on many covert operations becoming a skilled disguise women. At some point after the three way war between the [[Peacekeeper]]s, Seelie Court, and Unseelie Court, Freya was placed in [[Thieves Metropolis]] as a undercover agent. 
###### The Felwinter Saga
Freya was one of [[Titania, Queen of Seelie Court]]'s main intelligence and black ops agents, along with Mark and [[Daliah]]. She was on the same mission as Mark and Daliah when dealing with a criminal underworld league. Freya was also unaffected by the Pamnesia spell due to being under the Seelie Courts service (to Queen Titania). 
##### Abilities  
Freya is a level 15 Lightspeeder Wyrd
