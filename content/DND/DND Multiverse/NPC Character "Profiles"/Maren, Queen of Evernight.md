##### Brief History/Timeline   
The Queen of Evernight is the ruler of the City of Evernight. However, when Dox Vulethix discovered Evernight, he impregnated Maren, having her birth children [[Reemus, the Inscrutable Spider]], [[Shadow of Evernight]], and Meleena ([[Meleena's Wetnurse]]). Because of this unwanted pregnancy, the queen spiraled into disappear. She eventually, along with the rest of the City of Evernight, became trapped within [[Sai Mona]]'s dream.
