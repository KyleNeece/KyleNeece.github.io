##### Brief History/Timeline   
Reemus the Inscrutable Spider is one of the three Great Old Ones who was the offspring of Dox Vulethix (Outer God Great Old One), and The Queen of Evernight, Maren. Reemus the Inscrutable Spider lives in a endless lake, and its existence is the only thing holding the bloodmoon and reality of Evernight back from the Eldritch horrors.
