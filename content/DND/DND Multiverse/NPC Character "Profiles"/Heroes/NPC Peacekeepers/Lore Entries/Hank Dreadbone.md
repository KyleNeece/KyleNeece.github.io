##### Personality 
Hank, despite the name, is actually laidback, eccentric, and a jokester. Despite his appearance as a skeleton, he is a pretty likable guy and people enjoy being around him, even if the vibe is a little strange because he's a talking skeleton. In combat, he uses dark curse magic to his advantage
##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Adolescence 
Not much is known about Hank's adolescence, all that is known is that he died at a very young age and was magically resurrected by his curse for unknown reasons. He also bears the spirit of his dead brother from his past, who guides him throughout his life.
###### Twin Prodigies Arc
Hank Dreadbone was one of the main exchange students that came for a semester abroad at [[Peacekeeper Academy]], along with the rest of the party ([[Johnny Sigiliph]], [[Laila Iceburn]], [[Malatar Wütend]], [[Ryū Hoshi]], and [[Lillith Ashford]]) that would become rivals with [[Jackson Felwinter]]'s crew ([[Azriel Darkstar]], [[Aria Hassan]], [[Olivia Birdsong]], [[Emily Sigiliph]], and Yasu-2). Hank helped [[Yasu-2]] disperse the party from their pointless trash talk banter, as he was getting tired of it and he found it a drag like Yasu-2 did.
###### House Tournament Arc
Hank Dreadebone, like the rest of Jackson's and Johnny's group, finished very high in most of the events. He however, lost to Emily Sigiliph, as her raw fighting prowess overtook his curse and antimagic abilities.
###### The Illithid Mage Arc
During [[Arangol]]'s first invasion, Hank was one of the key students that drove off the invasion. He was able to combat against the high ranking mages within Arangol's force, almost nullifying them completely. After the invasion, Hank left to continue to embrace his curse even more, taking the time to grow it.
###### Betrayal Arc
When Azriel invaded, Hank immediately sought out Azriel, as he figured that he was the perfect counter to his skill set. However, Azriel also knew this, as he sent one of his best agents against Hank. When Hank faced off with one of his servants, he was defeated as his curse magic was greater then his.
###### Declaration of War Arc
Hank gathered the dead corpses of evil beings and shared his curse with them, reanimating them and brining them to the final battle, where he was one of the main special forces in battle tasked with clearing out the mages in Tartarus's force.
###### Panmnesia Arc
Hank was in the thick of battle when the Panmenesia spell hit, losing all of his memories.
