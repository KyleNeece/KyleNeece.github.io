##### Brief Summary 
Laila Iceburn is a Dragonborn Sorcerer originating from Faerun and is a S-Rank [[Peacekeeper]]
##### Appearance 
![[Laila Iceburn.png]]
##### Personality 
Laila is boisterous and talkative. She is interested in many other peoples cultures and enjoys learning about other's day to day lives. She shows a more fiery side when she is practicing something that she enjoys, and a more cooler side when she's with others and the vibe is less energy based. In combat, she typically resorts to massive magical explosions from her spells and blowing everything up. 
##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Adolescence
Laila had two Dragons as parents, however both of them were shapeshifted into humans at the time of her "creation". Laila was then birthed from her mother, who was confused about why she turned out as a Dragonborn. She was then raised among dragons, honing her sorcerer magic effectively and efficiently. Once she grew up, Laila was then determined and encouraged to attend the peacekeeper academy, where she can hone her craft and become a great adventurer. 
###### Twin Prodigies Arc
Laila Iceburn was one of the main exchange students that came for a semester abroad at Peacekeeper academy, along with the rest of the party ([[Johnny Sigiliph]], [[Malatar Wütend]], [[Ryū Hoshi]], [[Hank Dreadbone]], and [[Laila Iceburn]]) that would become rivals with [[Jackson Felwinter]]'s crew ([[Azriel Darkstar]], [[Olivia Birdsong]], [[Emily Sigiliph]], [[Yasu-2]], and [[Aria Hassan]]. Laila had a small conflict with Aria, which was broken up by Yasu and Hank. 
###### House Tournament Arc
Laila Iceburn, like the rest of Jackson's and Johnny's group, finished very high in most of the events. She however, lost to Yasu-2, as his magic was superior to hers
###### The Illithid Mage Arc
During [[Arangol]]'s first invasion, Laila was one of the key students that drove off the invasion. She made quick work for the many hordes of Necro and Host Walkers with her destructive magic 
###### Betrayal Arc
When Azriel invaded, Laila was too busy defeating the hordes of monsters to engage with Azriel or any high ranking general for that matter.
###### Declaration of War Arc
Laila gathered an army of Dragons and Drangonborn warriors to help fight in the big battle to save the world which she also partook in
