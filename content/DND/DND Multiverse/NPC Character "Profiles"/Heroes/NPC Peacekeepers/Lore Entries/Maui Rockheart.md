##### Brief Summary 
Maui Rockheart is a Oath of Heroism Paladin originating from the mountains of Icewind Dale, is second in command of the Healing Church, and a S-rank [[Peacekeeper]]

##### Personality 
Maui Rockheart is honorable, steadfast, competitive and has an unbreakable spirit. He is the light for those who are in the darkest of depths and makes effort to let his actions speak louder then his words. Maui loves to train, even using his spare rest time and days off in order to do so to keep his body and mind in peak condition. Maui is fierce yet performative in combat, almost treating it as a athletic performance. He uses his overwhelming strength and power to brute force his way through his enemies, smiting them in the name of heroism. 
##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Origins
Maui Rockheart grew up in the mountains of Icewind Dale in a Goliath tribe. The Rockheart clan is well known for their strength and endurance, but because of the relentless snowstorms and ice quickly learned that more was needed, a spirit of unbreakable resolve and honor. His childhood was filled with the stories of champions who sought the path of glory and heroism. Inspired by these stories, Maui trained his body and mind to the physical limits in order to become the heroes in the stories he was told. Once old enough, Maui set out to enlist in the peacekeeper force, hoping to become the stuff of champions and support his clan.
Maui Rockheart eventually ran into [[Alder]] in the Shadowfell within the [[City of Evernight]], who recruited the fellow Paladin to healing church. Due to his immense strength and power, he became second in command behind Alder
###### Recruitment
Maui's incredible feats of valor as well as his immense strength and power granted him the S-rank title
