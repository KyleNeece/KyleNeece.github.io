##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Adolescence
Twitch grew up in southern Faerun in a nomadic clan of great warrior Kenku's. The members of the tribe hailed from all different backgrounds, but they all shared in common a military background. The Kenku where warriors for hire, known as the watchful birds, however they would only serve those that they found were of good values and traits. Twitch at a very young age became the commanding strategic force in battle, directing his troops to victory in almost any battle. Twitch decided to leave the tribe, taking his talents to Sigil in order to use his tactical mind for good
###### Twin Prodigies Arc
Twitch had already known [[Jackson Felwinter]] before his time at the academy due to meeting him on Sigil when he was little. The two however moved with different circles, but were still very close friends regardless
###### House Tournament Arc
Twitch did very well in the house tournament, finishing high in all events. He lost in the combat tournament versus [[Emily Sigiliph]], who's combat prowess was greater then his, despite the incredible choreography of pure fighting skill between the two, making it the most entertaining fight in the tournament
###### The Illithid Mage Arc
Twitch was one of the first to act during the [[Arangol]] invasion. Taking quick command and controlling the battlefield. Even Arangol mentioned that his intelligence and strategy rivaled his, giving him praise for his high potential
###### Betrayal Arc
During [[Azriel Darkstar]]'s slow descent into evil due to Arangol's influence, Twitch returned to his homeland for a bit, gathering all the warriors from his old tribe together, he told them the situation and to stand by, as he suggested that another attack may be close. 
Surely enough, Azriel attacked, and Twitch and his elite forces made it back just in time in order to turn the tide of battle, minimizing casualties 
###### Declaration of War Arc
Twitch gathered legendary warriors far and wide, and they all agreed to temporarily be inducted into his court of heroes. Twitch amassed one of the greatest fighting forces the multiverse has seen simply from his tactics, his ability to network with high ranking warriors and combining with Emily Sigiliph's forces
###### Panmnesia Arc
Twitch was looking over the battle scene when the Panmnesia spell hit, losing all his memories thereafter
##### Abilities  
Twitch is a level 17 Warrior Dynasty Sovereign
