##### Brief Summary 
Kabe-44 is a Warforged Vanguard, a Sentient creation of [[Jotor Sparklefen]], and S-Rank [[Peacekeeper]]
##### Appearance 
![[Kabe-34.png]]
##### Personality 
Kabe-34 is a warforged of few words. He has the utmost respect for his master and Yasu-2 and will do anything to protect them. Kabe-34 while intimidating, has a brighter side, enjoying physical labor that involves using his immense strength to build things. In combat, Kabe-34 is described as an unstoppable force and immovable object. Rather then attacking the enemy directly, his goal is to use his extremely high defense to hamper foes and protect his allies, acting as a shield for all.
##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Origins
Having his memories stored and returned, Jotor created Kabe-34 as a defense mechanism as [[Yasu-2]] was not made for combat, more specifically, defense and offense through defense. Kabe-34 would normally stay at the shop with Jotor, until one day he was deemed ready to become a peacekeeper, only really taking on defensive or protective missions due to his linear and specialized purpose. 
###### Recruitment
[[Jackson Felwinter]] knowing about Jotor's machine building capabilities automatically admitted Kabe-34 into the force, giving him the S-rank title
##### Abilities  
Kabe-34 is a level 17 Juggernaut Vanguard
