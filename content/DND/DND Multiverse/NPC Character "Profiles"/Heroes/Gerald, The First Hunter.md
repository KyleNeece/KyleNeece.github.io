##### Personality 
Gerald is a older, wise and selfless man who puts himself and his duty of the hunt in front of others. He took the burden of Sai Mona's enslavement, making sure other hunters would not fall under his same fate. 
##### Brief History/Timeline   
Little is known about Geralds childhood. 
Gerald along with other Profane Soul Hunter's receive their powers from [[Sai Mona]] through the nightmare and the hunt.
Gerald when he was younger, ordered for the Extinction of the Order of the Mutant Blood Hunters (Including [[Ludwig Van Scythe]]) after their killing and harvesting DNA of the Great Old One washed up on the beach of a fishing village on the outskirts of Evernight.  
Gerald oversees the Hunt, which is a yearly occurrence within the City of Evernight due to Sai Mona's dream. 
##### Abilities  
Gerald is a level 16 Order of the Profane Soul Blood Hunter
