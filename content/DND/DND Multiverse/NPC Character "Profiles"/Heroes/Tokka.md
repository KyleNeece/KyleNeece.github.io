##### Brief Summary 
Tokka is a Circle of Stars Druid originating from Chult and is a S-Rank [[Peacekeeper]]. 
##### Appearance 
![[Tokka.png]]

##### Personality 
Tokka is a old, wise, intelligent and understanding Tortle who has a love for astronomy and the cosmos. Tokka protects those who cannot be protected, and is also a able physician, being a skilled healer as well. Tokka loves the stars so much that he has painted a mural of constellations inside of his shell, showing his expertise in works of art as well.
##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Adolescence
Tokka from a young age was a very creative and artistic kid. He loved to paint and one night, became inspired by the beautiful starry night sky, and began to paint it. Because of this early interaction with the starry night sky, he began to learn astronomy as he grew up, learning all the different constellations. These teachings were from his grandpa, who loved astronomy as much as Tokka did. One day, Tokka decided to venture beyond his homeland, and seek new adventure. His village helped him build a ship, and Tokka used the starry night sky to travel throughout Faerun.
###### House Tournament Arc
When Tokka arrived on Sigil to take a portal to visit his homeland, he saw the many advertisements about the Peacekeeper high tournament, he figured he'd go check it out briefly. When he arrived, the match between Ryū Hoshi and [[Azriel Darkstar]] just started. While watching the match, Tokka was intrigued by Hoshi, seeing that he also wielded cosmic power. However, it was not enough as Darkstar took the match, defeating [[Ryū Hoshi]]. After his wounds healed, Tokka made an effort to approach the young Pandaren, offering to mentor and train him in addition to his classes at the academy. Ryū accepted and became Tokka's disciple. 
###### The Illithid Mage Arc
Tokka was in the stands when [[Arangol]] launched his first invasion on sigil. Tokka quickly took action, focusing on taking out the Warlocks who posed threats to the blind spots of the melee fighters, while simultaneously healing the frontline from a distance. After repelling the invasion, Tokka then started his mentorship to train Ryū. 
###### Betrayal Arc
Tokka was training Ryū when Azriel invaded, seeking the last needed rune of the multiverse that was within Will Felwinter's Blade in order to complete his ascension to godhood. Tokka stood before Azriel himself and the two squared off. However, the fight did not last long or end well, as without backup Tokka was defeated by Darkstar as he continued to dominate Sigil's many warriors and make his way to central Sigil. Ryū rushed to the scene, just in time for Tokka to speak his last words of wisdom to him before his death
###### Panmnesia Arc
Tokka's spirit was reincarnated back into a new body (where his life was left off) by Jackson Felwinter's request to the Spirit Emperor, with the downside of Jackson's secret punishment
###### Recruitment
While Tokka is not technically on the S-Rank roster, he is still considered one, as he tends to have and continues to do so help the peacekeeper organization ever since training Ryū
##### Abilities  
Tokka is a level 16 Cricle of the Stars Druid
