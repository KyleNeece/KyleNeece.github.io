A peacekeeper is soldier of the peacekeeper force, who are the invisible protectors of the multiverse. The [[Peacekeeper Main Base of Operations]] is located on [[Sigil]]
###### **General Background**
After the [[2.6_Panmnesia Arc]], [[Jackson Felwinter]], who's simulacrum at this point was destroyed by [[Arangol]]'s avatar, established the Peacekeeper military force, which revived the idea of a Peacekeeper, and turned it into a militarized force that would end up doing his bidding and help him gain power and take over different planes. This would stretch his influence further, which would also help him with his ultimate plan of defeating [[The Omnipotent One]].
