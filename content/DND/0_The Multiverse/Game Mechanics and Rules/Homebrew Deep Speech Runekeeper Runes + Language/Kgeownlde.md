*Deep Speech (Knowledge)*

This weapon counts as a Magic rune. The creature wielding or carrying the inscribed object can cast Encode Thoughts  

**Invoking Time:** 1 action
**Duration:** 1 minute

For the duration, you can probe the surface thoughts of creatures within 30 feet of you (as per Detect Thoughts) and can cast Encode Thoughts as a free action once per turn while under this effect. You can only probe deeper into the thoughts of a creature if they are friendly towards you and receptively consents.

**Additional Effect**
- All-Originating: This rune can be used with any other language so long as the user is proficient in the aberrant language
