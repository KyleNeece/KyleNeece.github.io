##### Brief Summary 
Nathan Murgul is a Umbral Human Psion originating from the Underdark and formerly a S-Rank [[Peacekeeper Force]]
##### Appearance 
![[Nathan Murgal.png]]
##### Personality 
Nathan is very spontaneous and enjoys his own freedom. Despite this, he has a good sense of right/wrong and duty but prefers to solve problems his own way with free will versus other people commanding him, which is why he is typically works solo or freelances. 
##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Adolescence
Nathan Murgul grew up in the underdark, where he was a member of the Seldarine community. He stood out among the other's as most of the others who were in the community were either Duegar or Drow Elfs. What was unique about Nathan was his Psionic abilities, which tend to be rare. Nathan set out to become a peacekeeper to use his powers for better. 
###### The Felwinter Saga
Nathan Murgul left the peacekeeper force as he wanted more autonomy similar to his brother, but at the same time still wanted to do good. He then joined the Profane Soul Hunter's within Evernight, helping with the yearly hunts. He is aquatinted and friendly with [[Lady Gabriella Alucard II]], [[Alder]], [[Ludwig Van Scythe]], and [[Gerald, The First Hunter]], as he likes having a host of different social allies and callings, similar to his brother.
