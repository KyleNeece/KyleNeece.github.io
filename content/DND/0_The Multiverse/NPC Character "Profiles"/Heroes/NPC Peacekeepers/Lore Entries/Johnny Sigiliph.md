##### Brief Summary 
Johnny Sigiliph is a Sweet Science Human Pugilist and S-Rank [[Peacekeeper]]
##### Appearance 
![[Johnny Sigiliph.png]]
##### Personality 
Johnny Sigiliph's character on the outside looks average, when someone attempts to size him up he seems totally normal. Due to his strength, Johnny is very indifferent and goes through most things in life with little to no effort. Though he doesn't show strong emotions to others Johnny genuinely cares about people and has a good spirit. When someone close to him gets hurt or dies, he enters a state of tranquil anger and may even decide to not hold back in a fight.
##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Adolescence
Johnny Sigiliph grew up with his sister, [[Emily Sigiliph]], in a militaristic family. In his youth, Johnny was always considered weak and average, always losing to his sister in sparring and failing in his training. He accepted this, as he knew that he had to get stronger to become noticed and to defend himself. Everyday onward he did 100 pushups, 100 situps, 100 squads, and a 10 kilometer run. Eventually, he became the strongest in his family and any physical related activity became easy for him. He then set off for Peacekeeper academy as a transfer student, following his sister's footsteps in a wish to be challenged. 
###### Twin Prodigies Arc
Johnny Sigiliph was one of the main exchange students that came for a semester abroad at [[Peacekeeper Academy]], along with the rest of the party that would become rivals with [[Jackson Felwinter]]'s crew (including [[Azriel Darkstar]], [[Olivia Birdsong]], Emily Sigiliph, [[Aria Hassan]], and [[Yasu-2]]. Johnny made friends with the [[Malatar Wütend]], [[Lillith Ashford]], [[Ryū Hoshi]], [[Hank Dreadbone]], and [[Laila Iceburn]]. During exchange student orientation, Johnny and his group approached Emily and her friends after an all school assembly that included introducing the exchange students. The two groups then became rivals, competing in everything.
###### House Tournament Arc
Johnny, like the rest of Jackson's and his group, finished very high in most of the events. He lost to his sister by disqualification in the Battle Madness Event because he refused to fight his sister saying it would've been a "drag". 
###### The Illithid Mage Arc
During Arangol's first invasion, Johnny was one of the key students that drove off the invasion. A gigantic mutated wendigo was spawned in as a distraction for [[Arangol]] to leave. This was the perfect matchup for Johnny, as his raw strength overtook it.  
###### Betrayal Arc
When Azriel invaded, he targeted Johnny with mages in order to play off is stupidity. He made illusions of soldiers and kept throwing annoying enemy types at him so that he was stalled and would not interfere with his plans.
###### Declaration of War Arc
Johnny went with his sister Emily back home and called upon her family and their armies in order to protect [[Sigil]], he was one of the main frontliners, who would clear a path for Jackson to get to Azriel
