##### Brief Summary 
Lady Gabriella Alucard II is a Vampire Gunslinger originating from the Shadowfell and S-Rank [[Peacekeeper]]
##### Appearance 
![[Lady Gabriella Alucard II.png]]
##### Personality 
Lady Gabriella is egotistical and freely taunts in battle, both allies and foes. Sometimes she will let her enemies destroy her, just for her to regenerate herself to counter attack. She expresses extreme disgust over her kind that kill for no reason. She fights with cruelty and ferocity. Underneath al of this, Gabriella is sad and envious of humans, having great respect for humans who endure old age and death 
##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Adolescence
Nothing is known about Gabriella's childhood
###### The Felwinter Saga
Gabriella throughout the entirety of the Felwinter saga was dead, having existed way before the time of events. She was the second Dracula (considered as Draculina), originally holding immense power, and committing heinous acts. It was said that [[Ludwig Van Scythe]] and [[Alder]] engaged in combat with her, ultimately losing but gaining her respect. This respect stemmed from the two of them maintained humanity and would to not give up.
Upon a group of peacekeepers stumbling upon her coffin, she awakened once again, however, not hostile towards them. She had become more humane toward the end of her lifetime from spending more time around humanity. While this has made her weaker, it is best not to underestimate her as she a very formidable foe. She calls Ludwig and Alder her master, and will serve their every command in unyielding loyalty. 
###### Recruitment
Gabriella enlisted into the peacekeeper force, [[Jackson Felwinter]] automatically gave her an S-Rank grade due to reading about the legends that turned out to be reality.
