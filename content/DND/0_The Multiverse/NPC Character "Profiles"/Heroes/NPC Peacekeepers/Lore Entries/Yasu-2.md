##### Brief Summary 
Yasu-2 is a Autognome Peace Cleric, a Sentient creation of [[Jotor Sparklefen]], and S-Rank [[Peacekeeper]]

##### Personality 
Yasu-2 is tranquill and honest, always providing insight to whatever person or situation may need it. It often attempts to spread the word of Eldath, hoping for a kinder and more peaceful existence. However, since obtaining "peace" can happen in multiple ways, Yasu-2 is not opposed to combat, as sometimes the best way to spread peace is to silence those who won't comply.
##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Orgins
Yasu-2 was made by Jotor Sparklefen as a result of finding a way to create a sentient  being through artifice. Having succeeded and then some, desipte Yasu-2 being extremely young, he is extremely smart and insightful, due to Jotor imbuing fragments of knowledge into him during his creation.
###### Twin Prodigies Arc
Yasu-2 arrived to Peacekeeper high and made an effort to go up to Jackson and Azriel, greeting them warmly. The three stood out a lot, but mostly Jackson and Azriel as the other students could tell that they just defeated a minor threat in Sigil. During the house selection ceremony, Yasu-2 was sorted into House Equinox.
###### House Tournament Arc
Yasu-2 was able to score very high in all of the events due to his nigh perfect craftsmanship. 
During the House Tournament Arc, Yasu-2 was able to finish very high in the Social Networking challenge due to his ability to spread the word of his god and enforce peace among the citizens.
In the Gem Hunt Challenge, Yasu was top 3 in speed when finding the gems, as his excellent pathfinding skills were able to get him what he needed
In the Battle-Madness Challenge, Yasu-2 made it to the final rounds, only losing because he could not defend himself against Ryū's offensive capabilities.
###### The Illithid Mage Arc
Yasu-2 was in the stands when Arangol attacked. Yasu-2, seeing the opponent was an illithid lich, instantly discarded all moral and began to fight, thinning the undead minion horde quickly. Arangol saw this as a problem however, as he attempted to strike down Yasu-2, landing a few successful spells leaving him in critical condition. As Arangol was about to strike him down, Jackson blocked the attack, and told Lexi to grab and protect him.
###### Betrayal Arc
Yasu-2 was the second to sense the evil that now possessed Azriel. His preceptive nature caught Lexi constantly asking about Azriel's psychological state, which lead him to use his heightened divine power within him to check for anything corrupt. Surely enough, traces of Arangol were found, before he could preform exorcism it was too late, as Azriel disappeared from the academy, only to return later on. 
Yasu-2 attempted to stop Azriel, who was now Tartarus, but his attempts were futile, as he was sealed away in a specially designed extra-dimensional cube that imprisoned him from dispelling the evil mind control. In order for Yasu-2 to not participate in future battles, Azriel took him back with him after his invasion, as he saw Yasu-2 as a huge threat, even bigger then that of Jackson Felwinter.
###### Declaration of War Arc
Jackson was able to unseal Yasu-2 and the two immediately went to old friends for help. While Jackson gathered a lot of Will's old party members, Yasu-2 using his creator's memories, began activating the various contingencies he made in case something like this had happened. 
When war began, Yasu-2 was one of the main leaders of the main infantry force, using his god's divine power to help clear the masses. When he made it to Arangol's generals, he called upon his god directly and he answered his prayers, granting him indomitable strength and was even able to make it to Azriel.
However, it was still not enough, as Azriel was able to take him down once again.
