The Mask of Personas is [[Olivia Birdsong]]'s signature magic mask
###### Abilities 
Mask of Many Persona's gives a +1 to your proficiency bonus
- Targets of your spells that inflict charm have disadvantage on the saving throw. This effect applies only if the spell has a somatic or a material component
- You gain an extra use of bardic inspiration
- As an action outside of combat, the user can adopt a mask, each mask gives a different set of abilities depending on the mask chosen. You appearance changes to the corresponding mask. You gain a number of temp hp equal to (twice your bard level) for as long as you have the temporary hp or when you deplete it as a bonus action, you gain the following benefits associated with the mask you have chosen. You switch masks once per short rest or if you expend a bardic inspiration charge

| **Mask Name** | Effect                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| ------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Mage          | You gain the ability to cast the following spells<br>- Level 1: Magic Missile<br>- Level 2: Melf's Acid Arrow<br>- Level 3: Fireball<br>- Level 4: Ice Storm<br>- Level 5: Synaptic Static                                    |
| Warrior       | You gain the following features<br>- You attack twice instead of once whenever you take the attack action on your turn<br>- Your gain a +2 bonus to AC                                                                        |
| Ninja         | You gain the following features<br>- You become proficient in the stealth skill<br>- You can teleport in dim light or darkness and take the hide action as a bonus action                                                     |
| Healer        | You gain the ability to cast the following spells<br>- Level 1: Cure Wounds<br>- Level 2: Prayer of Healing<br>- Level 3: Revivify<br>- Level 4: Death Ward<br>- Level 5: Mass Cure Wounds                                    |
| Dancer        | You gain the following features<br>- You can give your action to another creature as an action<br>- You can use your reaction by making a performance (dex) check against the enemies attack roll to cause the attack to miss |
| Marksman      | You gain the following features<br>- You do not suffer disadvantage from long ranged attacks<br>- Your attacks ignore half and three quarters cover                                                                           |
| Noble         | You gain the following features<br>- You can use the help action as a bonus action from 30 feet away<br>- Whenever you make a d20 roll, you may add a d6 to the roll for every 200 gold pieces you spend                      |
