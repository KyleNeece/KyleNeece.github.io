The Battlerager's Cleaver is [[Gorlock (Rhulk)]]'s Battleaxe.
###### Abilities 
The Battlerager's Cleaver is a Magical Greataxe 
- The weapon deals 1d12 + strength modifier and has a +1 attack and damage modifier
	- Can cast False life at first level 1x/rest 
	- Whenever you use Reckless Attack with this weapon, you can forgo your advantage on the attack roll to swap the attack out for a cantrip
