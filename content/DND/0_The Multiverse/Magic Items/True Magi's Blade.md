True Magi's Blade is [[Jackson Felwinter]]'s modified Energy BowBlade

True Magi's Blade is a +3 weapon that can switch between a Finesse Longsword and a Longbow
- The +3 Weapon deals 1d8 + 2d6 Force Damage
	- **Modular Energy**: The sword's energy blade emits bright light in a 15-foot radius and dim light for an additional 15 feet. While the blade persists, you can use an action to expand or reduce its radius of bright and dim light by 5 feet each, to a maximum of 30 feet each or a minimum of 10 feet each.
	- **Dooming Critical**: Spellstrike can now critically hit, but you no longer add your intelligence modifier to the damage roll of the critical hit
	- **Unstoppable Lethality**: True Magi's Blade crits on a 19-20 and acts as a Vorpal Sword and a Sword of Sharpness. 

	- **Blade mode**
		- **Justice Strike**: You can cast Steel Wind Strike in Blade mode 1x per long rest. Whenever you cast Steel Wind Strike, you can target the same target multiple times and add your weapon damage and effects once per separate target to the damage rolls. 
		- **Absorbing Blade**: Whenever you are the target or in the area of effect of a spell, as a reaction you can attempt to absorb the spell by making a Arcana Skill check against a DC equal to 10 + the spell slot used when the spell was cast. On a success, you counter and absorb the spell. You can cast the spell through SpellStrike or restore a spell slot equal to the level of the spell cast to a maximum of level 5. The spell remains absorbed until the end of your next turn. You can use this ability a number of times equal to half your intelligence modifier rounded up and regain all expended uses on a long rest.

	- **Bow Mode**: 
		- **Puncture Volley**: You can cast Conjure Volley in Bow mode 1x per long rest. Whenever you cast Conjure Volley, you add your weapon damage and any effects to the damage roll
		- **Teleporting Shot**: When an arrow hits its target, you can teleport to wherever you fired this arrow. You can use this a number of times equal to your intelligence modifier and regain all expended uses on a long rest

	- **Both**
		- **Spell Storing**: This Weapon functions as a ring of Spell Storing with 5 charges.
		- **Tempus's Gifted Secrets**: At the beginning of each day, you may choose one spell from each level and spell list. You can cast each of these spells without expending a spell slot. Once you cast any of these spells in this way, you can't cast that spell in this way again for 24 hours. You can also cast these spells using spell slots you have of the appropriate level
		- **Deletion**: 3x per day you can slash or shoot anything and permanently remove a quality from reality (DMs discretion)
		- **Dimensional Cut and Pierce**: This Weapon can tear the fabric of reality, creating a temporary rift between planes. You can use your action to choose a different plane of existence from the one you’re on and slice or pierce through an unoccupied space within 5 feet of yourself, creating a rift to that other plane. The rift can be up to 10 feet high and 10 feet wide, and it lasts for 1 minute. Once this property is used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn.
