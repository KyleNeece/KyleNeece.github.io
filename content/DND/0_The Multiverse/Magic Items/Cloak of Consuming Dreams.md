The Cloak of Consuming Dream's is [[Aria Hassan]]'s signature magic item 
###### Abilities 
- The cloak gives a + 2 to your spell save DC and attack rolls
	- As an action you can regain a expended Warlock spell slot once per long rest 
	- While wearing the cloak in dim light or darkness, as a free action you can become heavily obscured in dim light and invisible in darkness to creatures who rely on darkvision. You gain advantage on stealth checks while in this state
		- As a bonus action you can teleport up to 30 feet into a shadow or area of darkness. You can use this a number of times equal to your charisma modifier and regain all uses on a long rest
	- Once per turn whenever you land an attack with a spell or weapon attack, the target takes an additional 3d6 psychic damage and must succeed on a saving throw vs your spell save DC or become frightened for 1 minute. A frightened creature that attempts to sleep that same day suffers horrific visions of Dendar, gaining no benefits from the rest
	- Whenever you kill a creature, you can extract their soul essence by making a DC15 Persuasion or Arcana Check. On success the spirit becomes stored in the cloak and is added to your menagerie. The spirit loyal to and. understands you, and it obeys your commands when summoned. Each day you can prepare a number of spirits from your menagerie equal to half your charisma bonus rounded up
		- Whenever you summon a creature using a spell or feature, you can replace the summon with one of the spirits you extracted, using the stat block of the spirit when it was alive
		- The cloak has 3 charges per day. As an action you can expend a charge to summon a creature from your collection of summons
