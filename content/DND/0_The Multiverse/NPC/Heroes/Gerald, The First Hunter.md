Gerald is the leader of the Order of the Profane souls within the [[City of Evernight]]
##### Brief Summary 
Gerald is a Human Order of the Profane Soul Blood Hunter
##### Appearance 
![[Gerald the First Hunter.png]]
##### Personality 
Gerald is a older, wise and selfless man who puts himself and his duty of the hunt in front of others. He took the burden of Sai Mona's enslavement, making sure other hunters would not fall under his same fate, but was ultimately fooled as his nightmare given by Sai Mona was the source of the cycle of the Evernight hunt. 
##### Brief History/Timeline   
Little is known about Geralds childhood. 
Gerald along with other Profane Soul Hunter's receive their powers from [[Sai Mona]] through the nightmare and the hunt.
When he was younger, Gerald ordered for the Extinction of the Order of the Mutant Blood Hunters (Including [[Ludwig Van Scythe]]) after their killing and harvesting DNA of the Great Old One washed up on a beach of a fishing village located along the outskirts of Evernight.  
Gerald oversees the Hunt, which is a yearly occurrence within the City of Evernight due to Sai Mona's dream. 
##### Abilities  
Gerald is a level 16 Order of the Profane Soul Blood Hunter
