##### Brief Summary 
Jotor Sparklefen is a Gnome Artificer originating from the plane of Eberron and is a S-Rank Peacekeeper
##### Appearance 
![[Jotor Sparklefen.png]]
##### Personality 
Jotor is a knowledgeable and crafty gnome who blends technology and magic at  high levels. He is always thinking about his next creation and new innovative ways to tackle problems. With the invention of Jotor's steel companion, he found the secret to imbuing machinery with life. While Jotor is normally cool headed and calm, he instantly blows comedic fuses when he is called little and is talked to in a taunting baby voice.
##### Brief History/Timeline  
###### Adolescence
Jotor was born in Ebberon, his love for artifice originated from his creativity as a kid. He was a mathematical genius, which is reflected in his ability to understand artifice. Jotor's parents loved him dearly as they lived a peaceful life on Ebberon. 
However sometimes with genius comes trouble, as sometimes Jotor was bullied in school as a kid. His dislike for those who bully and hurt others for fun inspired him to become strong through his intelligence. He used his artifice skills to create the foundations of what would later be his greater works.
###### Prelude
After fleeing into Ebberron and wandering with no purpose. [[Will Felwinter]] was mourning the loss of his best friend due to the surprise betrayal of Lady Pain. He stumbled across Jotor and his steel companion, who was defending himself from a circle of Assassins. While Will during this state would've normally turned his eye away due to his lost will to fight, he noticed that Jotor was fighting to protect, not to kill. 

Seeing this, Will helped Jotor, using the pommel of his broken Blade in order to defeat of the Assassins. After defeating the Assassins, and Jotor asked what happened to his blade. 

Due to rumors of Lady Pains actions combined with the broken Sunblade, Jotor instantly recognized the situation. He took Will back to his shop, and devised a plan to reconstruct the blade. Adventuring the Alara, the two traveled to the Alara santuary, in search of a fraction of the Gem of Alara. Arriving at a sacred village, the villagers were in despair as their resources had gone dry and growth had stopped as a result of Lady Pain's multiversal scale magic. Jotor, using his intellect and Will Felwinter's physical prowess in labor, was able to restore their land. The two were gifted a fraction of the Gem of Alara as a sign of thanks, allowing for Will to recraft his Sunblade.
###### Prelude Continued
Jotor helped participate in the final battle versus Lady Pain and her army. Jotor played a key roll in figuring our how to attack the center of Sigil. 
###### The Twin Prodigies Arc
After the events of the [[Epilogue to Cour 1 and Upbringing]] and before the [[2.1_Twin Prodigies Arc]], Jotor created Will's son, Jackson, a special Sunblade. 
Through his research after the battle for Sigil, Jotor found that new and different types of magic users began to appear across the multiverse. Curious about the phenomena, Jotor set out on an adventure to investigate. Under unfortunate circumstances, Jotor became lost, unable to find his way back to [[Sigil]].

###### Recruitment
Jotor was recruited near the end of his adulthood moving into his granhood. Despite his age, Jotor is still a adequate combatant and has not lost a step. Before the transfer of Peacekeeper ownership to Jackson Felwinter, Will recruited Jotor, who is now the lead weapon designer and artifact researcher of the organization.
