##### Brief Summary 
Kabe-44 is a Warforged Vanguard, Sentient creation of [[Jotor Sparklefen]], and S-Rank [[Peacekeeper]]
##### Appearance 
![[Kabe-34.png]]
##### Personality 
Kabe-34 is a warforged of few words. He has the utmost respect for his master and Yasu-2 and will do anything to protect them. While intimidating, Kabe-34 has a brighter side and enjoys physical labor that involves using his strength to build things. In combat, Kabe-34 is described as an unstoppable force and immovable object. Rather then attacking the enemy directly, his uses his extremely high defense to hamper foes and protect his allies, acting as a walking shield.
##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Origins
Having his memories stored and returned, Jotor created Kabe-34 as a defense mechanism. This is because [[Yasu-2]] was normally not made for combat. Kabe-34 would normally stay at the shop with Jotor, until one day he was deemed ready to become a peacekeeper. 
###### Recruitment
[[Jackson Felwinter]] knowing about Jotor's machine building capabilities automatically admitted Kabe-34 into the force, giving him the S-rank title
##### Abilities  
Kabe-34 is a level 17 Juggernaut Vanguard
