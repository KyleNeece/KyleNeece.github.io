##### Brief Summary 
Degalith, formerly known as the Human, Loranos Aryndor, is a Demon Lord that resides within the 54th layer of the Abyss.

##### Brief History/Timeline   
Degalith is a blood relative and former family member of House Aryndor. In his adolescence as a human by the name of Loranos Aryndor, he had good virtuous character and became a renowned warrior and Zodiac, known for his ethereal combat and his enlightened wisdom. However, at a time when war was no longer necessary, and House Aryndor became pacifists, Loranos angered. He brought his opinions to the house representatives, saying that conflict breeds the strong and that pacifism creates the weak. The house representatives discarded Loranos's opinion, which made Loranos seek power on his own. He began to grow more strong, learning ancient spells and magical abilities that would push him to his limits. House Aryndor accused him a threat, and sent a group of high assassins and mercenaries to kill him. In his last moments of life, Loranos sought an obsession for Wrath against all that scourged him. The beginning of this obsession lead to his birth in the Abyss after death, where he worked his way up the Demon hierarchy and obtained primordial catalysmic power and wrath, eventually leading to his rule in the 54th layer of the Abyss as the Demon Lord Degalith. The obsession finally lead him to his goal of full universal purging, starting with the 9 hells and ending with the edge of the universe. Some consider him to be natural disaster incarnated. 

He currently has [[Jackson Felwinter]] captured, and uses him as a infinite battery to power both his Demon army's new technology and his own powers and capabilities
