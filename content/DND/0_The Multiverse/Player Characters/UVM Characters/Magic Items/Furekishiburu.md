Furekishiburu is [[Equilibrium]]'s BoStaff 
###### Abilities 
Furekishiburu is a BoStaff
- The Bostaff deals 1d8 Bludgeoning + dexterity modifier damage
	- As a bonus action, you can gain a fly speed equal to your normal speed until the end of your turn. At the end of your turn and you are still in the air, you fall down, taking fall damage as normal. If you land on someone, you can deal 1d4 damage. Once you use this bonus action you cannot use it again until you finish a short or long rest
