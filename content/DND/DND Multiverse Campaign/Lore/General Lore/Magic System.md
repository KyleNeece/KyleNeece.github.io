Magic System 
- The weave 
	- All Magic comes from the weave, there are 3 types of weave: weave, shadow weave, and miracle weave
  

|            |                  |                                                                                                                                      |                                                                                                                                            |
| ---------- | ---------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Class**  | **Magic Family** | **How magic is casted**                                                                                                              | **Exceptions?, Details?**                                                                                                                  |
| Accursed   | Shadow Weave     | Curse bestowed upon them                                                                                                             | If their curse is fully removed, they will not have magic                                                                                  |
| Alchemist  | Miracle Weave    | Through combination of magical materials and by manipulating the laws of nature                                                      | They cannot cast magic themselves, only their alchemy can produce magical effects                                                          |
| Artificer  | Weave: Arcane    | Imbuing energies of the weave into objects                                                                                           | An Artificer must imbue their energies into an object or craft to use their magic                                                          |
| Bard       | Weave: Arcane    | Turning the weave energies into harnessable song                                                                                     | Through “Tuning the Weave’s energies” (like tuning an instrument) they can cast spells from the weave that originate from other sub-weaves |
| Cleric     | Weave: Divine    | As a reward to their devotion to a god, the deity grants them access to the weave                                                    | If a Cleric’s faith is abandoned, they will be magicless. Shar followers use Shadoweave                                                    |
| Druid      | Weave: Primal    | Through communing with the divine aspects of nature                                                                                  | Failure of communion or cooperation results in the failure to cast spells                                                                  |
| Merchant   | Weave: Arcane    | Taking an items valued properties and harnessing them as magic                                                                       | Items of value are needed in order to use their magic                                                                                      |
| Monk       | Miracle Weave    | Using their energies from within (ex: Ki or Chakra) in order to perform magical actions and feats                                    | Exhausting ones inner strength leaves them magic-less                                                                                      |
| Odic       | Weave: Divine    | Using the life force in them or around to use their magic                                                                            | If they use their full life force they die                                                                                                 |
| Paladin    | Weave: Divine    | Through agreeing to a set of beliefs and convictions that align with their ideals and goals                                          | If the oath is broken they will become an oathbreaker, their magic will change                                                             |
| Psion      | Miracle Weave    | Through manifesting the power of fundamental force via thought                                                                       | A rarer magic, Psionics can surface through training, an accident, birthright, etc. so long as it was a miracle                            |
| Ranger     | Weave: Primal    | Through communing with the divine aspects of nature                                                                                  | Failure of communion or cooperation results in the failure to cast spells                                                                  |
| Runekeeper | Miracle Weave    | Through the magical properties of words and symbols which are inscribed on runes                                                     | Can use runes to tap into all types of magic depending on their natures                                                                    |
| Sculptor   | Weave: Arcane    | Through the manipulation of the ambient weave around them to make constructs                                                         | Failure to understand how to sculpt weapons will lead to failure of spellcasting                                                           |
| Shaman     | Weave: Divine    | Communal through spirit guides of the world as well as their teachings                                                               | Different Spirit Guides grant different magical powers                                                                                     |
| Sorcerer   | Weave: Arcane    | Through their bloodline, where a relative is either inherently magic with the weave or has been touched or exposed by a cosmic force | Despite being of bloodline, the magic can still be suppressed                                                                              |
| Warlock    | Weave: Arcane    | Through a signed contract, the patron grants the access to the weave                                                                 | If the contract is broken, they will lose all their magic until it’s amended or they make another contract                                 |
| Wizard     | Weave: Arcane    | Studying the weave’s properties and directly/forcefully manipulating its energies                                                    | If the spellbook is lost, the wizard will have to get a new one in order to re-study the spells to prepare new ones                        |
| “Witch”    | Weave: Arcane    | Either from birthright or from hard study work of the weave (similar to either sorcerer or wizard)                                   | Potential of magic is the same be it from birthright or study                                                                              |
| Wyrd       | Miracle Weave    | Through the chaos and fates of the multiverse they can use unique “magic” powers                                                     | The magic power manifested is random                                                                                                       |
| Zodiac     | Weave: Divine    | Through the study and embracement of a Zodiac celestial shape which gives you knowledge of the weave                                 | Different celestial shapes give different magical effects                                                                                  |
