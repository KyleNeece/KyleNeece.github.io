##### Brief Summary 
The Omnipotent one is a Disembodied School of Chronomancy Wizard and formerly helped the [[Peacekeeper]] force
##### Appearance 
![[The Omnitemporal One.png]]
##### Personality 
The Omnipotent one is very calm, collected and wise, often giving the best advice whenever it is needed, however, he does not know all, as even he is not the perfect being. He has been alive for eons, overseeing the timeline and making sure people don't mess it up, though he can meddle with it subtly. He is always rooting for humanity, hoping it will never go extinct. While he is proficient in pretty much all forms of magic, he is the best with time magic. He also helped Jackson Felwinter cast the Panmnesia spell, forgetting all of the events that happened throughout the Felwinter Saga Cour 2, and making sure to enlist in the peacekeeper force as an overseer
