##### Brief Summary 
Ryū Hoshi is a Sign of the Dragon Pandaren Zodiac from the far away land of Kara-Tur and S-Rank [[Peacekeeper]] 
##### Appearance 
![[Ryū Hoshi.png]]

##### Personality 
Ryū Hoshi is courages, honorable, loyal and believes in the justice of the blade. Ryū is always looking for his next duel, always wanting to test his strength and abilities of both the blade and the magic of the stars. Ryū also enjoys painting good works of art and constellations, exercising the art of Bun and Bu. Ryū enjoys a good friendly competition, always looking for others to push him beyond his limits and vice versa
##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Adolescence
Ryū Hoshi was a young boy who loved the stars and the stuff of legends and heroes. In his small village he grew up being told stories, myths, and other forms of fantasy about mighty heroes, evil villains and their clashes, Ryū rigorously practiced his swordsmanship, quickly becoming a deadly combatant with the Katana, even learning techniques that could slice through the toughest of material, which most Katana's could not. Ryū wanted more however, as he wished for some sort of divine power. Through studying the constellations of the cosmos for long and hard enough, he finally obtained the power of the Dragon Constellation. Ryū went abroad to peacekeeper academy years later, to test his might versus the best. 
###### Twin Prodigies Arc
Ryū Hoshi was one of the main exchange students that came for a semester abroad at Peacekeeper academy, along with the rest of the party that would become rivals with Jackson Felwinter's crew. Ryū also made the wish to Azriel to test his blade vs his might
###### House Tournament Arc
During the house tournament arc, Ryū scored very high in all of the tests. Scoring high in diplomacy and gem hunt, and even making it to the final rounds of the combat tournament. 
Ryū gave Azriel a hard time, pushing him back early. However, Azriel kept holding back, not wanting his Abyssal powers to go out of control. After Azriel's defeat, Ryū was frustrated, as he sensed that Azriel had greater power but he did not want to use it. Ryū's battle with Jackson Felwinter was cut short due to Arangol's first invasion. 
###### The Illithid Mage Arc
During Arangol's first invasion, Ryū Hoshi was one of the key students that drove off the invasion. His prowess with the blade combined with his dragon themed astrologer skills allowed him to be a frontline warrior who's blade was able to cut down multiple foes at once. After the battle, Ryū sought Tokka, wishing for him to train him in the arts of astrology. Tokka agreed to the accord, and began training Ryū
###### Betrayal Arc
When Azriel invaded, Ryū helped to combat the invasion and minimize casualties. Towards the end of the fight, he finds a dying Tokka, who dies in front of him from grievous wounds caused by Azriel 
###### Declaration of War Arc
Ryū Hoshi gathered the Pandaren people from Kara-Tur, and partook in the big battle to save the world
###### Panmnesia Arc
Ryū Hoshi was finishing off the final forces of the enemy when the Panmnesia spell was cast, losing all his memories of the events
###### Recruitment
Ryū Hoshi was auto admitted into the peacekeeper force due to his raw power and was given S-Rank
##### Abilities  
Ryū Hoshi is a level 17 Sign of the Dragon Zodiac
