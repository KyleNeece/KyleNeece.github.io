##### Brief Summary 
Sisters of Fallen Sanctity is a hybrid [[Great Old One]] Angel fusion of the two angels Lilith and Na'ameh

##### Brief History/Timeline   
The two sisters at some unknown time, despite their already descent with their Fallen Angel status, sought more power. The two found a unknown tome of Eldritch Knowledge, now lost, and read its continents. Instead of dying, the two ended up fusing together along with the Eldritch magic they discovered, becoming a monstrosity of a creature, who would wander aimlessly throughout the universe with no purpose. They were unable to have any sort of goal or direction according to observers. 

At some point in time, the creature made it to Thilvatov, a somewhat out of the way planet, also the original home to Monty.
