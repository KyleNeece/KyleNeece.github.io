##### Brief History/Timeline

###### Origins/Adolescence

###### The Illithid Mage Arc and Betrayal
At some point in time during his universe crusade, Tartarus (see [[Azriel Darkstar]]) traveled to Dominaria as his very first plane, and didn't take it over until later. It was here he received training from [[Arangol]], but also at the same time, had a child he didn't even know about.

###### Declaration of War Arc and Panmensia Arc
Tartarus didn't dare go back near go back towards Dominaria, even at full strength, as they housed some of the most powerhouse of beings in the universe, so he sent his second in command instead, only to die to Karn. This was when Nyleth was still young and as such did not take part in the defense versus the invasion. 
Before the Panmnesia spell, Teferi phased out the entirety of the plane, and it came back into existence later in the timeline. 

###### Bridge between Cour 1 and Cour 2 
Before [[Jackson Felwinter]]'s capture in the Abyss, he journeyed to Dominara at the report (by Aria, Aria went with him as well) of a large threat to the universe. At the time Nyleth was young, and apart of a orphanage that also served double as a religious church and community. When Jackson "assassinated" the pope during a service on a special holiday event, chaos ensured and a battle within the church occurred. Nyleth sought battle with Jackson (Jackson was happy to see Nyleth, as he thought he had no more family after Azriel died). Upon Jackson being stabbed with Nyleth's **Special sword** and being completely fine, Nyleth realizes that Jackson only killed demons. 

###### Omnipresent War Arc

###### Campaign 2
At some point, by orders of his uncle, Nyleth was instructed to trace the murders of a few members of the Dryden clan (one of the four naturally powerful human clans, the others being Felwinter, Efrideet, and Forge). Jackson gave him Aeris's information if he wanted help on the case, and while Nyleth didn't really feel the need from a combat perspective, he thought his wisdom, experience, and another brain would be helpful. He sought out Aeris, and the two began their investigations.
