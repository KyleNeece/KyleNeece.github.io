##### Brief Summary 
Maui Rockheart is a Oath of Heroism Paladin originating from the mountains of Icewind Dale, is second in command of the Healing Church, and a S-rank [[Peacekeeper Force]]
##### Appearance 
![[Maui Rockheart.png]]
##### Personality 
Maui Rockheart is honorable, steadfast, competitive and has an unbreakable spirit. He is the light for those who are in the darkest of depths and makes effort to let his actions speak louder then his words. Maui loves to train, even using his spare rest time and days off in order to do so to keep his body and mind in peak condition. Maui is fierce yet performative in combat, almost treating it as a athletic performance. He uses his overwhelming strength and power to brute force his way through his enemies, smiting them in the name of heroism. 
##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Origins
Maui Rockheart grew up in the mountains of Icewind Dale in a Goliath tribe. The Rockheart clan is well known for their strength and endurance. Because of the relentless snowstorms and ice, the tribe learned that a spirit of unbreakable resolve and honor is necessary to survive harsh challenges. His childhood was filled with the stories of champions who sought the path of glory and heroism. Inspired by these stories, Maui trained his body and mind to the physical limits in order to become a hero in the stories he was told. Once old enough, Maui set out to enlist in the peacekeeper force, hoping to become the stuff of champions and support his clan.
Maui Rockheart eventually ran into [[Alder]] in the Shadowfell within the [[City of Evernight]], who recruited the fellow Paladin to healing church. Due to his immense strength and power, Maui became second in command behind Alder. With his devotion to the church, Maui works as a "agent" for the church who monitors and gathers information about the Peacekeepers for the church.
