Sunblade of the Worthy is [[Jackson Felwinter]]'s modified Sunblade Multiweapon

Sunblade of the Worthy is a +3 weapon 
- The +3 Longsword deals 1d8 + 2d6 Force Damage
	- **Modular Energy**: The Sunblade can turn into an Bow and Vice versa 
	- **Unstoppable Lethality**: Sunblade of the Worthy crits on a 19-20 and acts as a Vorpal Sword and a Sword of Sharpness 

	- **Blade mode**
		- **Justice Strike**: You can cast Steel Wind Strike in Blade mode 1x per long rest. When you cast Steel Wind Strike this way, you can target the same target multiple times and add your weapon damage once per separate target to the damage rolls. 
		- **Absorbing Blade**: Whenever you are the target or in the area of effect of a spell, you can attempt to absorb the spell by making a Arcana Skill check with the DC equalling 10 + the spell slot used when the spell was cast. On a success, you absorb the spell and can cast the spell through SpellStrike. The spell remains absorbed until the end of your next turn. 
		- 
	- **Bow Mode**: 
		- **Puncture Volley**: You can cast Conjure Volley in Bow mode 1x per long rest. When you cast Conjure Volley this way, you add your weapon damage to the damage roll
		- **SpellShot**: You can use Spellstrike with this ranged weapon
		- 

	- **Both**
		- **Dimensional Cut and Pierce**: This Weapon can tear the fabric of reality, creating a temporary rift between planes. You can use your action to choose a different plane of existence from the one you’re on and slice or pierce through an unoccupied space within 5 feet of yourself, creating a rift to that other plane. The rift can be up to 10 feet high and 10 feet wide, and it lasts for 1 minute. Once this property is used, it can’t be used again until the next dawn.
		- **Deletion**: 3x per day you can slash or shoot anything and permanently remove something from reality (DMs discretion)
