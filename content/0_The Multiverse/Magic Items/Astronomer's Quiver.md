Astronomer's Quiver is a Quiver that produces special arrows that align with the 12 Zodiac Signs on the Quiver. 

Sign ideas : 
- The Hunter (precision) --> higher accuracy  
	- Advantage on the attack roll when shooting this arrow
- The Maiden (life) --> shoot allies to heal
	- Shoot this arrow at an ally and deal no damage. Grant hp to ally equal to damage rolled
- The Giant (power) --> more damage
	- On hit all targets in a 10 foot radius from creature take additional 1d6 damage and must make a DC15 strength save, on a fail a be knocked prone
- The Owl (wisdom) --> shoot target and casts a divination spell on them or wisdom stuff
	- Shoot this arrow at an ally to grant a bonus to wisdom skills and saving throws 
- The Sentinel (protection) --> taunt effect
	- When hits, target, target has disadvantage on all attack rolls against creatures except for you until the start of its next turn
- The Vizard (treachery) -->  mind control?
	- Shoot arrow at a hostile creature and when hit they consider you an ally 
- The Pariah (loss) --> penalty 
	- When hit, the target cannot benefit from healing and has disadvantage on charisma checks 
- The Hammer (justice) --> marked
	- When hits a target they become under the effect of the command spell, you choose the command  
- The Wayfayer (curiosity) --> teleporting arrow
	- Can shoot the arrow and teleport to its location 
- The Lantern (comfort) --> dispels all darkness and obscuring conditions in an area
	- When shot it emits a 20 foot radius of light that dispels all darkness and obscuring effects
- The Oracle (fate) --> 
	- Shoot an arrow to see 1 day into the characters future
- The Abyss (fear) --> frightens 
	- Ignores resistances, when shot, target has to make a dc10 saving throw or automatically die
