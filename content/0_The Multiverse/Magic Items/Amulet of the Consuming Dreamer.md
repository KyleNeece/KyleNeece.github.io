You could take some inspiration from Bloodborne, in that (spoilers) Great Ones yearn to create children, and yet cannot conceive them within their own planes because their children always inevitably destroy the parent. As such, they reach out across space and time both to try to sow the seeds where their young might be raised far, far away from themselves.

As such they need a "surrogate" to prepare the plane for the arrival (or creation?) of their young. Communication is difficult, because of how alien they are, as well as the fact that they might be long dead (or not even born yet themselves, as time is malleable for beings of such power). So at first the messages are muddled, but as the Warlock grows in power they learn that the Great One's goal is to transform them into something _other,_ something _unrecognizable,_ something _perfect_ for raising an infant Great One during the short amount of time that it is vulnerable after its birth into the plane.

These revelations might be horrifying to the warlock when discovered, but the Great One's gift is given lovingly, genuinely. After all, is there no greater kindness than elevating a creature so insignificant that it can, for the briefest of instants, participate in a process infinitely greater than itself?
