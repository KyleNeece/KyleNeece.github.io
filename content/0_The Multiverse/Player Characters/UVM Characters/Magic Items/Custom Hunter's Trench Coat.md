The Custom Hunter's Trenchcoat is a magic item gifted to  [[Monkey (Alex)]], [[Aeris Aryndor]], [[Otto]], [[Jaloop]], [[Monty]], [[Akanos]], [[Equilibrium]], [[Chuck]], and [[Gorlock (Rhulk)]] from [[Ludwig Van Scythe]]. 

The user can use their bonus action or action to transform the Trench Coat into any wearable apperance they desire (DMs discretion)
