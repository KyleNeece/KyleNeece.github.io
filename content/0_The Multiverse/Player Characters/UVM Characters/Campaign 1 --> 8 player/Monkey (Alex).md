##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Adolescence
When Monkey was young, Monkey loved to fight, it was his passion as it was a way to exercise and stay active within the deep forests of Zendikar. However, when he was finally old enough to understand that his father was abusing his mother, Monkey was strong enough to where he could face his father and protect his mother. Unknowing of his strength, Monkey killed his father, even though his intentions were to not to. Because of this, and his father being a higher up in their tribe, he was banished from his homeland and was stripped of his name. And sought a new life to live. He eventually stumbled upon a stray dog named dog, and since the two were vagabonds, they made a strong friendship very quickly. 
 > [!Warning]- Spoilers Here Click at Own Risk 
> It was later found out that the dog was a manifestation of Alex's Willpower and raw strength, and it broke off from his body because his will to live was so strong
###### The Peacekeeper Trials Arc
Monkey participated in the peacekeeper trials in order to become enlisted within the force. He and 7 others were assigned to [[Ludwig Van Scythe]], who oversaw their trial. Completing the trials, Monkey was assigned the D letter peacekeeper rank, and would eventually work his way up into the higher tiers. 
###### The Evernight Hunt Arc
Monkey was apart of the group that was assigned to the Shadowfell with Ludwig and [[Lady Gabriella Alucard II]], for the purposes of claiming land and building influence among the plane. The party journey throughout the wilds of the shadowfell before making it to the [[City of Evernight]]. They were able to take out one of [[Arangol]]'s researchers, the [[Landlord]], the keeper of the magical mansion. After defeating him and destorying the [[Necrowalkers]] and [[Hostwalkers]] laboratory, turning the estate into the peacekeeper force, the party continued to push forward, eventually making it to a graveyard, where one of [[Nathan Murgul]]'s estates is located. The party pushed forward into the city after a brief meeting with Nathan, learning more about the area and smaller details about the city. Monkey and the party entered the city, after getting a taste of what true madness felt like after smelling some of the white orchids that they picked from the field leading to the bridge. After a few strange dreams, the party made it into the city, and heard church bells ring as the Evernight Hunt began. After dispatching a few monsters, a profane soul hunter lead the group back to their Workshop to meet [[Gerald, The First Hunter]], who would induct them into their hunter ranks to fight off the beasts of the night. 

Monkey and the party continued on through the City of Evernight, they met a little girl by the name of [[Anna Efrideet]], who was home alone with her sister due to her mother going out to find their father. After, the eventually encountered their first problematic beast, Ben Efrideet, who transformed into a beast due to the blood curse and killed his wife. After a hard fight, the part was able to kill the frenzied and transformed profane soul hunter. The party made their way back to the house, finding a crying Anna who killed her sister as she was transforming. Taking the girl with them, Monkey and the party made it to the District of Healing. Upon arrival to the Healing Church, the party meet [[Alder]], a Devotion Paladin of Selune. Alder allowed for the party to stay within the church, as it was a safe place where people could rest and stay. 

The party continued on deeper into the city. Eventually making it to a Magical Wendigo, who was guarding a magical skull. After making work of the Wendigo, the party touched the magic skull. It held more of [[Otto]]'s memories, and it revealed to the group that Otto was defeated by Jackson Felwinter. Upon this realization, Alucard II and Alder meet them with hostility, evaluating whether or not Otto would be a threat to the force. Otto choose to be civil, and the party then continued on through the City of Evernight, eventually making it to Castle Cainhurst. They party met the newest ruler, [[Ariana Draculina]]. They also found out that Otto was the third ruler, and the one before him was Alucard, but the time gap between the rulings was extensive. 

After their depature from Castle Cainhurst, the party continued through the city, eventually making it to a small sub district within the City. the party met [[Anna Efrideet]]'s aunt, Elle, and the party helped in stoping the [[Shadow of Evernight]] from continuing to terrorize the town. Upon his defeat, Tartarus became stronger, and learned the Abberant Rune Language. The party then made it to a lake, where [[Reemus, the Inscrutable Spider]] inhabited. Upon it's defeat, the party triggered the blood moon and lifted a lot of the obscuring magic that [[Sai Mona]] had cast. The party then made their way through the unseen district, and defeated the [[Trapped Souls of Evernight]]. A portal then opened leading to the nightmare of Sai Mona, to which the players entered. The party made a long and difficult journey to the top of a tower, where [[Meleena's Wetnurse]] was located and a projection of [[Maren, Queen of Evernight]] stood in dispair. Uponn the Wetnurses defeat and Maren's lifted grief, the party then made it back to the workshop. 

At the workshop Gerald wanted to free them of the hunt, but before he could do so Ludwig interfered, after a back and fourth of dialogue, the party found that Gerald was being controlled by Sai Mona unwillingly and was the host of the dream. Upon the party and Ludwig defeating Gerald, [[Sai Mona]] descended, and the party did battle. [[Jaloop]], [[Chuck]], and [[Ludwig Van Scythe]] perished, but in Ludwigs last words he gave the party a choice: two scrolls of pure resurrection. Ludwig then passed, and Jaloop and Chuck were saved. Jaloop absorbed Sai Mona's essence, and the hunt came to an end. Alder took Anna Efrideet and the Healing Church Paladins to Sigil, and the party completed their mission. 
###### Ghost Treason Arc
 A few months after the hunt, Monkey (now known as Alex) and Aeris, returned to Sigil, after the two had been helping the poor in their time off. Their rank had been considerably risen because of such actions. After a short reunion, Alex and the party went to the Multiversal Tavern, where they relaxed and indulged in their services. After a plethora of interesting events, the party then got ready for Ludwig's funeral. After the service, [[Maui Rockheart]] gave them Ludwigs key to his office. In the office, the party found many things, but the most important being the recipe and requirements for the Ritual of Soul Reincarnation. The party was also assigned a new regiment leader, [[Emily Sigiliph]]. The party then ventured to the Feywild with her, as she said that she knew that Ludwig was trying to resurrect [[Azriel Darkstar]], and that she was willing to pick up his work where he left it off. Upon arriving to the Feywild, she entrapped the party within a barrier, and revealed that she had been working for [[Arangol]]. After Monty nearly killing everyone from an explosion (though it was later revealed that Emily didn't actually die...), he was incapacitated by Aeris's parents. Who took them to a base of operations where [[Emily Sigiliph]], [[Yasu-2]], and [[Olivia Birdsong]] additionally where awaiting their waking as well. Emily then explained that she had took part-time leave from the peacekeeper force to work for Arangol as a mercenary so that she could track his movement and plans better. Because of her temporary leave and the completing of her contract, Emily is currently in freelance, and her along with the other allies agreed to help the party with their next journey through the Feywild.
