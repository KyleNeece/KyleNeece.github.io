Abberant Runes is the runic language of Aberrant creatures. It is said to be the predecessor to all the magical Runekeeper Runes (See [[Magic System]])
###### **Kgeownlde**
- All-Originating:
	- This rune can be used with any language so long as the user is proficient in the aberrant language 
- This rune counts as a Magic Rune. The creature wielding or carrying the inscribed object can cast Encode Thoughts  

- **Invoke: 1 Action**
- **Duration: 1 minute**
- For the duration, you can probe the surface thoughts of creatures within 30 feet of you (as per Detect Thoughts) and can cast Encode Thoughts as a free action once per turn while under this effect. You can **only** probe deeper into the thoughts of a creature if they are friendly towards you and receptively consents.

###### Tartarus's Benevolence
*Abberant (Shield, Elder)* 
*Prerequistite: None*
- Gift from a Friend:
	- This rune can be used with any language and is permanently inscribed. It does not count against the number of inscribed and/or attuned runes you can have. 
- This rune counts as a Ward rune. The attuned creature has advantage on saving throws against effects that inflict conditions. 

- **Invoke: Special**
- **Duration: Instantaneous
- When you fail a saving throw, you can invoke this rune to succeed instead (no action required)
	- Once you invoke this rune, you must spend 4 runic charges to do so again

*Azriel's descent to evil was a product of his powerless and depressive feelings, slowly killing him on the inside. When Arangol molded Tartarus from Azriel's destroyed character, Tartarus misguidedly sought a free will and no evil reality under the evil influence of Arangol. It was only after Tartarus's soul resurrected did he begin to care for others once again, leading to Tartarus's sacrifice that would not only rebirth Azriel, but most importantly.... save a friend.*
