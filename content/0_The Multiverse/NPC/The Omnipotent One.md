##### Brief Summary 
The Omnipotent one was a Disembodied School of Chronomancy Wizard and formerly helped the [[Peacekeeper]] force
##### Appearance 
![[The Omnitemporal One.png]]
##### Personality 
The Omnipotent one is very calm, collected and wise, often giving the best advice whenever it is needed, however, he does not know all, as even he is not the perfect being. He has been alive for eons, overseeing the timeline and making sure people don't mess it up, though he can meddle with it subtly. He is always rooting for humanity, hoping it will never go extinct. While he is proficient in pretty much all forms of magic, he is the best with time magic. He helped [[Jackson Felwinter]] cast the Panmnesia spell. After this, he sealed himself away in order to contain his growing power and stabilize the realms.
##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Origins
Nothing is known about the Omnipotent one, all that is known is that he was fond [[Jaloop]]'s magic one day, and he granted him magical music and spells, and has existed for eons of time.
##### Abilities  
The Omnipotent one was a Divination Wizard who ascended to Godhood and became a deity
