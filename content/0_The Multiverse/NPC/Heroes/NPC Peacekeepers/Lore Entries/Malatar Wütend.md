##### Brief Summary 
Malatar Wütend is a Half-Orc Barbarian originating from Faerun and is a S-Rank [[Peacekeeper Force]]
##### Appearance 
![[Malatar Wütend.png]]

##### Personality 
Malatar is tempestuous but also unintentionally witful and stoic at rare times. Malatar's tempestuous side only shows when in battle, or when diplomacy (which he is bad at usually) does not go his way. Malatars combat ability, while reckless and less skill oriented, is fearsome to go against, as he enjoys getting hit as much as he likes dealing the hits. Some think he is a psychopath when he starts laughing when blood spills profusely, whether its his or his opponents
##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Adolescence
Malatar grew up within the northern lands of Faerun, Moonsea. Through primal instinct, wit, and will, Malatar and his family learned to survive and make a living off of their victories through violence. The many battles and hardships developed his stoic personality. Malatar became a fierce warrior who protected his family whenever one sought harm to them. Through their victories in battles, Malatar set out for [[Sigil]] as an exchange student, to become a even greater warrior
###### Twin Prodigies Arc
Malatar Wütend was one of the main exchange students that came for a semester abroad at Peacekeeper academy, along with the rest of the party ([[Lillith Ashford]], [[Johnny Sigiliph]], [[Hank Dreadbone]], [[Laila Iceburn]], and [[Ryū Hoshi]]) that would become rivals with [[Jackson Felwinter]]'s crew ([[Azriel Darkstar]], [[Aria Hassan]], [[Olivia Birdsong]], [[Emily Sigiliph]], and [[Yasu-2]]). Malatar noticed immediately that Jackson and Azriel were strong, and instantly went up to them trying to goad them to fight. Ryū Hoshi told him to wait however, as they will get their chance in the tournament and to be honorable at the same time in the moment.
###### House Tournament Arc
Malatar Wütend, like the rest of Jackson's and Johnny's group, finished very high in most of the events. He however, he lost to Jackson Felwinter, as his reckless raw strength was not enough for him to defeat Jackson's Finesse and Magical capabilities. 
###### The Illithid Mage Arc
During [[Arangol]]'s first invasion, Malatar was one of the key students that drove off the invasion. Malatar became excited and struck down foe after foe. Malatar then spent his time taming his inner rage and brute strength, learning from his fight with Jackson that sometimes brute strength was not enough to win a fight.
###### Betrayal Arc
When Tartarus invaded, Malatar immediately attempted to seek him out, as he wished to test his newfound skill versus him. However, Tartarus had planned for this too, and sent swarms of forces after Malatar to stall him, as he had to way to deal with multiple crowds of enemies by himself effectively, which stalled him until he left Sigil with his goal completed.
###### Declaration of War Arc
Malatar gathered all the Orc tribes he knew, telling them that this was their time to test their strength and power in battle. Malatar led the Orc battalion in the war to protect the planet
