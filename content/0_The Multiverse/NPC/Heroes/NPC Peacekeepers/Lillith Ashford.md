##### Brief Summary 
Lillith Ashford is a Bladed Bravado Elf Dancer originating from Evereska and S-Rank [[Peacekeeper]]
##### Appearance 
![[Lillith Ashford2.png]]![[Lillith Ashford.png]]

##### Personality 
Lillith has a gentle and warm personality, which also augments her appearance. She is soft spoken, polite and caring, who uses honorifics when addressing anyone, including the enemy. She has a great sense of duty and order. With her grace, she lets others know that while she is soft, she is not someone to mess with, which ends up striking fear into the her fellow colleagues. However, if situation gets dire, she will revert to a violent and bloodthirsty nature, being a cold blooded killer. 
##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Adolescence
Lillith grew up in Evereska, where she lived a relaxing life with her family, she grew up in a higher noble background. Despite this, she was still warm to all no matter their economic status. Her father who was apart of the Swords of Evereska taught her the art of the sword, and she instantly fit in with the regiment. What made Lillith unique was her style, as her mother was an elegant dancer, which she also taught Lillith, turning her into a beautiful killer. Lillith was sent to the Peacekeeper academy in order to test her abilities against people from other planes
###### Twin Prodigies Arc
Lillith Ashford was one of the main exchange students that came for a semester abroad at Peacekeeper academy, along with the rest of the party ([[Johnny Sigiliph]], [[Malatar Wütend]], [[Ryū Hoshi]], [[Hank Dreadbone]], and [[Laila Iceburn]]) that would become rivals with [[Jackson Felwinter]]'s crew ([[Azriel Darkstar]], [[Olivia Birdsong]], [[Emily Sigiliph]], [[Yasu-2]], and [[Aria Hassan]]. Lillith made quick friends with Olivia
###### House Tournament Arc
Lillith Ashford, like the rest of Jackson's and Johnny's group, finished very high in most of the events. She however, lost to Emily Sigiliph, as Emily was more of a true fighter with overwhelming strength versus Lillith who was more finesse like. 
###### The Illithid Mage Arc
During [[Arangol]]'s first invasion, Lillith was one of the key students that drove off the invasion. In that moment, she changed from her warm and caring personality to the blood thirsty nature that she adopts during her missions as a Sword of Evereska. After the fight, she continued on with her daily training back home during the break period 
###### Betrayal Arc
When Azriel invaded, Lillith spent a lot of her time protecting Olivia, as she was too traumatized to do anything.
###### Declaration of War Arc
Lillith Ashford gathered the Army of Evereska to help fight in the big battle to save the world which she also partook in
