##### Brief Summary 
The Trapped Souls of Evernight is a abomination created from the despair of the dead people who live in the [[City of Evernight]]

##### Brief History/Timeline   
The Trapped Souls of Evernight was created from the manifestation of the dead citizens despair and anger against [[Sai Mona]] who trapped all of Evernight within a never-ending dream.
