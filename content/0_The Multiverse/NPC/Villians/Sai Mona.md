##### Brief Summary 
Sai Mona is a Outer God [[Great Old One]], and responsible for the Hunt and trapping all of the [[City of Evernight]] within a repeating cycle dream

##### Brief History/Timeline   
Sai Mona was jealous of Dox Vulethix and his ability to have children and ascend into a higher plane of existence. Because of this, Sai Mona decided to trap all of the [[City of Evernight]] within a dream, in order to stop  evolution and wanted humanity to kill Meleena ([[Meleena's Wetnurse]]), so that she could not only spite Dox Vulethix, but also transcend herself and the plane itself to a higher level of existence. She trapped [[Gerald, The First Hunter]] into a endless nightmare unknowingly due to the fishing village incident, which then resulted in him becoming her slave, and preparing hunters to hunt.
