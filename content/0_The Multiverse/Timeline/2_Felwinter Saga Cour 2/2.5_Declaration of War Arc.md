The Declaration of War Arc is a part of the second cour of the Felwinter Saga taking place after the [[2.4_Betrayal Arc]], following Jackson Felwinter and his party through their adventures in [[Sigil]]

###### Prelude 
Following the events of [[Azriel Darkstar]] and [[Arangol]]'s invasion, Sigil had to rebuild their city and peoples faith in protection of the universe. Multiple characters departed on personal journeys. 

###### Early 
[[Twitch]] called upon [[Jackson Felwinter]], [[Emily Sigiliph]], [[Aria Hassan]], [[Yasu-2]], [[Olivia Birdsong]], [[Lillith Ashford]], [[Laila Iceburn]], [[Johnny Sigiliph]], [[Ryū Hoshi]], [[Malatar Wütend]], and [[Hank Dreadbone]], to serve as part of his "royal court" as a Warrior Dynasty Sovereign, which formed [[The 12 and the King]] unformally. They all agree, and Twitch planned on serving as the main general for the coming war. Jackson sought out his father's old friends who rallied people from around the universe to help; Aria went to her peoples for help; Yasu sought all followers of Eldath to help, and Emily sought her parents's army to help. The rival group also went on their own journeys to gather forces for the coming war

###### Middle 
Many people of many backgrounds and upbringing gathered on Sigil as one, ready to combat the forces of Evil that was Azriel and Arangol's army of Eldritch beings, [[Hostwalkers]], and [[Necrowalkers]]. The city made sure to equip civilians and warriors alike with the adequate equipment for the fight ahead. 

###### End
Azriel, Arangol, and their infantry and generals gather onto Sigil through a portal, and war begins.
