##### Brief Summary
Fwip is a Kenku and was originally apart of the Fire Opal Devils company who defected the blood war. 

##### Appearance


##### Origins
Being a member of the Abyss's forces, Fwip was originally an enemy of the Devils within the blood war. After being defeated on a mission by Joshua, he was then captured until he was allowed to join him as part of his company, the Fire Opal Devils. He was then placed in the company along with [[Fwip]], [[Fedrett Benedict]], [[Elaran Polarshard]], [[Helter Skelter]], and [[Claymore]]. These were the main members, along with many other high capability soldiers. After a raid in the abyss went south, Fwip defected, as despite living the encounter Joshua fled, essentially disbanding the team. He then became a hitman and rebel, only taking jobs and targeting corrupt individuals who go against the law.
