##### Brief Summary
Joshua Price, also known as the Phantom Devil is a human fighter and was originally apart of the Fire Opal Devils company who defected the blood war.

##### Origins
Joshua Price was birthed from the old project Sigiliph where Zariel attempted to clone Sigiliph DNA and create super soldiers. While the DNA wasn't perfect, it gave each child enhanced physical and mental capabilities, and were deemed "the perfect soldiers". He was then released into the world until he was automatically inducted into the blood war once he became of a grown age. He was then placed in a company along with [[Fwip]], [[Fedrett Benedict]], [[Elaran Polarshard]], [[Helter Skelter]], and [[Claymore]]. These were the main members, along with many other high capability soldiers. After a raid in the abyss went south, Joshua fled, and became a bounty hunter to keep making a living.
