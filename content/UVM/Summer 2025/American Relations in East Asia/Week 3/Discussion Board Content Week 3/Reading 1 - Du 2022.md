**The Peking Rape Incident... Section**
- On September 1, 1946, a civil servant’s wife in the capital of Nanjing was chased by GIs on her way home after watching a late show, then was raped outdoors and injured
- On August 1, 1947, a factory worker in Qingdao was gang-raped by four American servicemen and pushed down a hill, after they had chased away her eleven-year-old son, who had been calling desperately for help 
- On August 7, 1948, an orchestrated gang rape took place at the end of a fare- well dance party in Wuhan, perpetrated mostly by American merchants and officers
