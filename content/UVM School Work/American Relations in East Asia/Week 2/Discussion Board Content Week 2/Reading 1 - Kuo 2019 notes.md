**Conclusion Section**
- "Orphan imagery reinforced a certain view of the missionary project, validating and confirming the premise of Chinese need and American benevolence. Images of impoverished Chinese children depicted the horrible conditions under which Chinese children lived before missionary intervention and functioned as pleas for American aid. In terms of their ideological effects, the images depicted the suffering the children encountered and reinforced the notion that Chinese children needed to be rescued by lofty American missionaries, who offered them spiritual salvation as well as material benefits." pp. 26
