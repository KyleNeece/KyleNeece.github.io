*Deep Speech (Steadfast, Elder)*
*Prerequisite: Nine other inscribed runes, none of which can be an Elder rune.*
*Item: A suit of armor (requires attunement)*

The rune of one of the two twin prodigies, the prince of Nephalems who was reborn anew. You you cannot be compelled to do anything against your will 
Further, as a bonus action you can compel a creature to face off against you and mark it as your target. Until the start of your next turn, that target has disadvantage on any attack roll that isn't against you. Additionally, the target is forced to make a intelligence saving throw against your rune magic save DC. On a fail, until the start of your next turn whenever the creature deals damage to anyone besides you, once per turn they take your choice of psychic, radiant, or fire damage equal to your intelligence modifier. 

**Invoking Time**: Special 
**Duration**: Instantaneous

When you fail a saving throw, you can invoke this rune to succeed instead (no action required).
Once you invoke this rune, you must spend 5 runic charges to do so again.
