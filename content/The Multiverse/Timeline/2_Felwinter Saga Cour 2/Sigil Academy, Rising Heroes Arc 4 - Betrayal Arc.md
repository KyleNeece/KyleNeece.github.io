The House Tournament Arc is a part of the second cour of the Felwinter Saga taking place after the [[Sigil Academy, Rising Heroes Arc 3 - The Illithid Mage Arc]], following Jackson Felwinter and his party through their adventures in [[Sigil]]

###### Prelude 
The Peacekeeper Academy students took their final final exams for the year, and finished their first year of [[Peacekeeper Academy]]. 

###### Early
However, the interplanar police issued an order to keep all residents of Sigil on the planet. This was not actually the police, but rather [[Arangol]] pupeting them, as Sigil had the strongest fighters in the universe so his plan was to conquer the planet will killing every threat that would make him and [[Azriel Darkstar]]'s crusade more difficult. 

###### Middle
Jackson and [[Aria Hassan]] were visiting her village, when Aria felt a strange magical presence on herself similar to that of Azriel. 

After obtaining 6 out of the 7 runes of the Multiverse, Azriel began his crusade on Sigil. This was a surprise attack and the entirety of Sigil scrambled to fight. 

When Azriel returned to Sigil, Aria's mind was taken control by Azriel, and she began wrecking carnage among sigil. It was only when Jackson appeared did he stop her and lift the spell did Aria then figured out that it was not Azriel's own will, but rather Arangol had given Azriel false experiences, making it seem like he was operating on his own free will when in reality it was Arangol's spell that was subtly mind controlling him. 

On Azriel's crusade, [[Tokka]] stood between him and his goal, saying he will not allow for him to pass. Azriel, too immensely powerful at this point for the Tortle made quick work of him, effortlessly defeating and inflicting fatal wounds that would later kill him.

Upon Azriel returning to Sigil, [[Emily Sigiliph]] was pissed. As Azriel led his forces into the capital, Emily dashed to the scene, taking out many of Azriel's forces on the way. However, once she reached Azriel, he taunted her, leaving her to disregard all strategy and rush at him, causing him to swiftly dodge and counter attack, leaving her with a fatal wound. Azriel went off to obtain the final multiverse rune in order to complete his ascension.

[[Yasu-2]] attempted to stop Azriel, who was now [[Tartarus Enslaver of Worlds.jpg]], but his attempts were futile, as he was sealed away in a specially designed extra-dimensional cube that imprisoned him from dispelling the evil mind control. In order for Yasu-2 to not participate in future battles, Azriel took him back with him after his invasion, as he saw Yasu-2 as a huge threat, even bigger then that of [[Jackson Felwinter]] due to his innate ability to become an avatar of Iallanis. This would ultimately spell Azriel defeated later. 

Azriel made it to the rune, however, [[Olivia Birdsong]] stood before him, sobbing and asking Azriel why he is doing what he is doing. Azriel tells her that he is going to reconstruct the multiverse, where everyone can live in harmony and no evil can exist. Olivia then makes the point that no one is truly free if they are controlled by fear, as she tells him to stop one last time and that he has broken her heart. Azriel, having totally lost himself, attempts to kill her to put her out of her psychological misery, only to be blocked and challenged by Jackson Felwinter, who asks him why he is doing what he is doing. 

The two clash, causing the world to shake. In a close but long fought battle, Azriel finally strikes down Jackson, and forcefully takes the rune for himself from a paralyzed from sorrow Olivia, completing his ascension to a greater being and fully becoming Tartarus. As he is about to enslave the planet, he realizes that his invasion timer runs out, and is teleported back to Arangol's base of operations.  

###### End 
After the invasion, everyone who participated in the battle had to have their nigh fatal wounds checked, help dispose of dead bodies, or rebuild the city.
