Magic System 
- The Veil 
	- All Magic comes from the Veil, there are 3 types of Veil: The Veil, Shadow Veil, and Miracle Veil

|            |                  |                                                                                                                                     |                                                                                                                                         |
| ---------- | ---------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Class**  | **Magic Family** | **How magic is casted**                                                                                                             | **Exceptions?, Details?**                                                                                                               |
| Accursed   | Shadow Veil      | Curse bestowed upon them                                                                                                            | If their curse is fully removed, they will not have magic                                                                               |
| Artificer  | Veil: Arcane     | Imbuing energies of the Veil into objects                                                                                           | An Artificer must imbue their energies into an object or craft to use their magic                                                       |
| Bard       | Veil: Arcane     | Turning the Veil energies into harnessable song                                                                                     | Through “Tuning the Veil’s energies” (like tuning an instrument) they can cast spells from the Veil that originate from other sub-Veils |
| Cleric     | Veil: Divine     | As a reward to their devotion to a god, the deity grants them access to the Veil                                                    | If a Cleric’s faith is abandoned, they will be magicless. Shar followers use ShadoVeil                                                  |
| Druid      | Veil: Primal     | Through communing with the divine aspects of nature                                                                                 | Failure of communion or cooperation results in the failure to cast spells                                                               |
| Merchant   | Veil: Arcane     | Taking an items valued properties and harnessing them as magic                                                                      | Items of value are needed in order to use their magic                                                                                   |
| Odic       | Veil: Divine     | Using the life force in them or around to use their magic                                                                           | If they use their full life force they die                                                                                              |
| Paladin    | Veil: Divine     | Through agreeing to a set of beliefs and convictions that align with their ideals and goals                                         | If the oath is broken they will become an oathbreaker, their magic will change                                                          |
| Psion      | Miracle Veil     | Through manifesting the power of fundamental force via thought                                                                      | A rarer magic, Psionics can surface through training, an accident, birthright, etc. so long as it was a miracle                         |
| Ranger     | Veil: Primal     | Through communing with the divine aspects of nature                                                                                 | Failure of communion or cooperation results in the failure to cast spells                                                               |
| Runekeeper | Miracle Veil     | Through the magical properties of words and symbols which are inscribed on runes                                                    | Can use runes to tap into all types of magic depending on their natures                                                                 |
| Sculptor   | Veil: Arcane     | Through the manipulation of the ambient Veil around them to make constructs                                                         | Failure to understand how to sculpt weapons will lead to failure of spellcasting                                                        |
| Shaman     | Veil: Divine     | Communal through spirit guides of the world as well as their teachings                                                              | Different Spirit Guides grant different magical powers                                                                                  |
| Sorcerer   | Veil: Arcane     | Through their bloodline, where a relative is either inherently magic with the Veil or has been touched or exposed by a cosmic force | Despite being of bloodline, the magic can still be suppressed                                                                           |
| Warlock    | Veil: Arcane     | Through a signed contract, the patron grants the access to the Veil                                                                 | If the contract is broken, they will lose all their magic until it’s amended or they make another contract                              |
| Wizard     | Veil: Arcane     | Studying the Veil’s properties and directly/forcefully manipulating its energies                                                    | If the spellbook is lost, the wizard will have to get a new one in order to re-study the spells to prepare new ones                     |
| “Witch”    | Veil: Arcane     | Either from birthright or from hard study work of the Veil (similar to either sorcerer or wizard)                                   | Potential of magic is the same be it from birthright or study                                                                           |
| Wyrd       | Miracle Veil     | Through the chaos and fates of the multiverse they can use unique “magic” powers                                                    | The magic power manifested is random                                                                                                    |
| Zodiac     | Veil: Divine     | Through the study and embracement of a Zodiac celestial shape which gives you knowledge of the Veil                                 | Different celestial shapes give different magical effects                                                                               |
