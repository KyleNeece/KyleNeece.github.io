##### Personality 
Gabe is a pompous prince who thinks he deserves a higher status. He’s constantly looking for validation of his royal authority and aura. Otherwise he’s quite fun-loving and playful. He doesn’t take much seriously and rarely gets his hands dirty. Almost always fighting from range. Which works because he’s a naturally excellent marksmen.
