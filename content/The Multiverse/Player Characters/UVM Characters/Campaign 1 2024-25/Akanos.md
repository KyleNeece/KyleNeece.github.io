##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Adolescence
Akanos and his parents grew up in the Hells, and are descendents of Asmodeus. When the blood war broke out, Akanos was told to flee as his parents were enlisted in the war. From then on, Akanos had to make a living for himself within Baldur's Gate. His talents for thieving, deception, and bits of magic landed him within the Nine Fingers Guild, where he then partook in the thieves guild activities. Akanos once he was old and skilled enough set off for bigger ambitions, attempting to join the peacekeeper force and make a name for himself. 

###### The Peacekeeper Trials Arc
Akanos participated in the peacekeeper trials in order to become enlisted within the force. He and 7 others were assigned to [[Ludwig Van Scythe]], who oversaw their trial. Completing the trials, Akanos was assigned the D letter peacekeeper rank, and would eventually work his way up into the higher tiers. 

###### The Evernight Hunt Arc
Akanos was apart of the group that was assigned to the Shadowfell with Ludwig and [[Lady Gabriella Alucard II]], for the purposes of claiming land and building influence among the plane. The party journey throughout the wilds of the shadowfell before making it to the [[City of Evernight]]. They were able to take out one of [[Arangol]]'s researchers, the [[Landlord]], the keeper of the magical mansion. After defeating him and destorying the [[Necrowalkers]] and [[Hostwalkers]] laboratory, turning the estate into the peacekeeper force, the party continued to push forward, eventually making it to a graveyard, where one of [[Nathan Murgul]]'s estates is located. The party pushed forward into the city after a brief meeting with Nathan, learning more about the area and smaller details about the city. Akanos and the party entered the city, after getting a taste of what true madness felt like after smelling some of the white orchids that they picked from the field leading to the bridge. After a few strange dreams, the party made it into the city, and heard church bells ring as the Evernight Hunt began. After dispatching a few monsters, a profane soul hunter lead the group back to their Workshop to meet [[Gerald, The First Hunter]], who would induct them into their hunter ranks to fight off the beasts of the night. 

Akanos and the party continued on through the City of Evernight, they met a little girl by the name of [[Anna Efrideet]], who was home alone with her sister due to her mother going out to find their father. After, the eventually encountered their first problematic beast, Ben Efrideet, who transformed into a beast due to the blood curse and killed his wife. After a hard fight, the part was able to kill the frenzied and transformed profane soul hunter. The party made their way back to the house, finding a crying Anna who killed her sister as she was transforming. Taking the girl with them, Akanos and the party made it to the District of Healing. Upon arrival to the Healing Church, the party meet [[Alder]], a Devotion Paladin of Selune. Alder allowed for the party to stay within the church, as it was a safe place where people could rest and stay. 

The party continued on deeper into the city. Eventually making it to a Magical Wendigo, who was guarding a magical skull. After making work of the Wendigo, the party touched the magic skull. It held more of [[Otto]]'s memories, and it revealed to the group that Otto was defeated by [[Jackson Felwinter]]. Upon this realization, Alucard II and Alder meet them with hostility, evaluating whether or not Otto would be a threat to the force. Otto choose to be civil, and the party then continued on through the City of Evernight, eventually making it to Castle Cainhurst. They party met the newest ruler, [[Ariana Draculina]]. They also found out that Otto was the third ruler, and the one before him was Alucard, but the time gap between the rulings was extensive. 

After their depature from Castle Cainhurst, the party continued through the city, eventually making it to a small sub district within the City. the party met [[Anna Efrideet]]'s aunt, Elle, and the party helped in stoping the [[Shadow of Evernight]] from continuing to terrorize the town. Upon his defeat, Tartarus became stronger, and learned the Abberant Rune Language. The party then made it to a lake, where [[Reemus, the Inscrutable Spider]] inhabited. Upon it's defeat, the party triggered the blood moon and lifted a lot of the obscuring magic that [[Sai Mona]] had cast. The party then made their way through the unseen district, and defeated the [[Trapped Souls of Evernight]]. A portal then opened leading to the nightmare of Sai Mona, to which the players entered. The party made a long and difficult journey to the top of a tower, where [[Meleena's Wetnurse]] was located and a projection of [[Maren, Queen of Evernight]] stood in dispair. Uponn the Wetnurses defeat and Maren's lifted grief, the party then made it back to the workshop. 

At the workshop Gerald wanted to free them of the hunt, but before he could do so Ludwig interfered, after a back and fourth of dialogue, the party found that Gerald was being controlled by Sai Mona unwillingly and was the host of the dream. Upon the party and Ludwig defeating Gerald, [[Sai Mona]] descended, and the party did battle. Jaloop, [[Chuck]], and [[Ludwig Van Scythe]] perished, but in Ludwigs last words he gave the party a choice: two scrolls of pure resurrection. Ludwig then passed, and Jaloop and Chuck were saved. Jaloop absorbed Sai Mona's essence, and the hunt came to an end. Alder took Anna Efrideet and the Healing Church Paladins to Sigil, and the party completed their mission. 

###### Ghost Treason Arc
A few months after the hunt, Akanos continued his peacekeeper duty, becoming a substitute peacekeeper under [[Lillith Ashford]] and [[Hank Dreadbone]]. After a short reunion, Jaloop and the party went to the Multiversal Tavern, where they relaxed and indulged in their services. After a plethora of interesting events, the party then got ready for Ludwig's funeral. After the service, [[Maui Rockheart]] gave them Ludwigs key to his office. In the office, the party found many things, but the most important being the recipe and requirements for the Ritual of Soul Reincarnation. The party was also assigned a new regiment leader, [[Emily Sigiliph]]. The party then ventured to the Feywild with her, as she said that she knew that Ludwig was trying to resurrect [[Azriel Darkstar]], and that she was willing to pick up his work where he left it off. Upon arriving to the Feywild, she entrapped the party within a barrier, and revealed that she had been working for [[Arangol]]. After Monty nearly killing everyone from an explosion (though it was later revealed that Emily didn't actually die...), he was incapacitated by Aeris's parents. Who took them to a base of operations where [[Emily Sigiliph]], [[Yasu-2]], and [[Olivia Birdsong]] additionally where awaiting their waking as well. Emily then explained that she had took part-time leave from the peacekeeper force to work for Arangol as a mercenary so that she could track his movement and plans better. Because of her temporary leave and the completing of her contract, Emily is currently in freelance, and her along with the other allies agreed to help the party with their next journey through the Feywild. 

However, before Akanos could venture with the party into the Feywild, he was called from leave by Lillith Ashford and Hank Dreadbone. Akanos then went on missions of them instead. 

###### Omnipresent War Arc
The party begins Azriel's resurrection, only Queen Titania to show up and resurrect her Black Ops squadron. The party is outmatched, and frantically attempts to hold off Titania and her squadron until the process is done. In a close fight between the two (including the death of Aeris Arendor and Monty), the party was able to fully complete the resurrection of Azriel Darkstar. After the fact, Titania and Azriel squared off, with the party taking care of the rest of the forces. Upon defeating Titania, Azriel was able to discern that they were being mind controlled by some external force, and told the party to make their way to Sigil while he takes care of a few things. 

Upon returning to Sigil, Akanos along with the group were deemed the S-Rank title, and were instructed to visit Jackson Felwinter's office as a official rite of passage to becoming S-Rank. Upon making it to his office, Jackson congratulates them and assigns them to be his own personal squadron, and says he can provide anything they need up front. The party gives the orb they stole from Daliah in order to cure Monty of his disease, and Olivia Birdsong rushes into the office, having figured out the truth. Jackson then blitzes and impales her, revealing he is actually [[Arangol]]. Arangol offers them a seat and tea, explains that he was the one who created the Peacekeeper force, and that Jackson has been gone a long time. He reveals the truth of the past from Cour 2 of the Felwinter Saga, and shows the party that Jackson is trapped within a demon realm, and that his wife, [[Aria Hassan]] has been missing. As Arangol is about to execute the party, Azriel tackles bashes through the door and tackles Arangol out the window. Akanos and the party race to their car along with Emily, Yasu-2, and a dying Olivia. However they are not let off the handle easy, as enemy vehicles and forces begin chasing them as well. Upon making it to the portal, Gabriella Alucard, Gerald, Anna Efrideet, Freya, and Nathan Murgul arrive to the scene, and buy them time to open it and escape. The defense was a success, and the party jumps through the portal. 

The party is split and fell into the first of the nine layers of the hells. Aeris ended up with Akanos and a dying Olivia. The two put aside their differences and search for shelter first and foremost. Upon setting up for the night and resting, Akanos sleeps as Aeris takes first watch. He then wakes up to a women holding a knife to the throat of Aeris. Tt is none other then Aria Hassan. After Aeris explains himself, she passes out from exhaustion, after fighting for years in hell all alone with no allies to count on, an enemy of both devils and demons. Once she wakes up, Aeris, Akanos, and Aria make their way to the 9th layer of hell. After a brief battle with Asmodeus, Aria and Asmodeus agree to disagree, and the party goes out to defeat Degalith, who was revealed to be Aeris's cousin. The party went over the plan, which was for half to distract Degalith, and the other to invade his lair to free Jackson Felwinter, who had been trapped there for centuries. Upon making it to the lair, the party was tricked, and faced the true Degalith. Aeris was then able to attune to the Moonblade and achieve his angel form. Upon saving Jackson and escaping the Abyss, the party reunites with the other group. The two groups then set out to take back Sigil 

The party arrives as Sigil, where half fight of peacekeeper forces, and the other half (the PCs) fought Arangol. Upon defeating Arangol, the party was warned of something way bigger. 
After the fight, Akanos continued to work with Jackson Felwinter, and took place in the events of ___ Darkstar (see _ Darkstar's page). After a few years passed, Akanos was with Jackson and the peacekeepers when the [[The Infinite Reader]] and the [[The Istoria Rytsarey]] invaded. He was tasked on contacting the others while this was going on and then helped in attempting to save the city. 
After winning their respective battles, the party ventured to only find Jackson and Azriel brutally wounded and in a coma. The Infinite Reader then sought to team up with Arangol, who was trapped in a special hedron. As Arangol turns him down, he puts the Infinite reader under Narrative Change, manipulating the time he had to invade Sigil, and as the Infinite Reader returns, striking a deal to Monty, who then takes it and joins his Runekeeper people. Monty and the Infinite Reader then dissipate and returns to his parallel dimension. Distraught, Akanos helps the peacekeepers get ready to partake in the fight versus [[The Istoria Rytsarey]] on Sigil. 
Upon fighting through puzzles and significant enemies, the party makes it right before the lair. The party is then split, and each member faces off against one of the Elites. Akanos faced off against the Fable of Destruction. He was able to put up a good fight long enough to where the Hunters of Evernight, the Healing Church, and Seelie court black ops would take care of the job from there, so that he could help face off against The Infinite Reader. 
The party and Monty, who revealed that his whole plan was to double cross the Infinite Reader, make it to the throne room and face off against him. As the party is about to die, Arangol uses his Narrative Change, turning the tide, and Jackson and Azriel return. Upon a hard fight, Monty shooting a Anti-Runekeeper bullet, Jackson; Azriel; and Arangol using their respective final blows, and Akanos's final hit, The Infinite Reader dissipates. A door shows in reality, and the party enters through to meet [[The Creator]]. As Jaloop and Monty fill in The Infinite Reader and the [[The Omnipotent One]]'s respective duties (Monty raced Azriel, giving Azriel a new chance at life and essentially sacrificing himself), the party returns to Sigil, and a celebration is held

###### Epilogue
Akanos attended Azriel and Olivia's wedding. After ruling his kingdom for many years, he later died of old age (since he did not have all the traits of a Vampire) with those who he loved around him, no longer feeling alone but rather, loved. A funeral was held, by which Aeris and Otto attended (Alex was now bound in hell, and Jaloop and Monty could no longer interact with the world directly). His scarab now lives on with Jackson, who takes good care of it for the sake of its old owner.
