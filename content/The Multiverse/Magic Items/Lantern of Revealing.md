While lit, this [hooded lantern](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Items:hooded%20lantern?expansion=34047#content) burns for 6 hours on 1 pint of [oil](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Items:oil?expansion=34047#content), shedding [bright light](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Rules:The%20Environment?expansion=34047#toc_3) in a 30-foot radius and dim light for an additional 30 feet. [Invisible](https://roll20.net/compendium/dnd5e/Rules:Conditions?expansion=34047#toc_7) creatures and objects are visible as long as they are in the lantern's bright light. You can use an action to lower the hood, reducing the light to dim light in a 5-foot radius.

**Known Possessors**
- [[Emily Sigiliph]] (permanent eye augmentation)
