The Peacekeeper Communication Device is a variable magic item that can take the form of any accessory per the wearers choice 
- While you touch one device, you can use an action to cast the _[sending](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:sending)_ spell from it at will. The target is the another designated bearer of a Peacekeeper Communication device. You can communicate with multiple people at once. If no creature bears the device, you know that fact as soon as you use the device and don't cast the spell.
- Moreover, you can cast illusionary script at will, and communicate over long distances with it in the same way you communicate with the sending spell

**Known Possessors**
- All [[Peacekeeper]]s
