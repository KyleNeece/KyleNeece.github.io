Astronomer's Quiver is a Quiver that produces special arrows that align with 12 Zodiac Signs on embroidered the Quiver. 

- The quiver has 1d4 charges at the start of each day, the charges reset every 24 hours. At the beginning of the day, roll on the D12 Zodiac table. The quiver gains a number of the Arrow type rolled on the table for the next 24 hours equal to the D12 roll, where after it disappears. 
	- When you make an attack roll, you can expend a charge to shoot the Arrow Type gained for the day.
