Bahamuts Mythical Figurine (Dragon) is a gift from Bahamut to Jackson Felwinter. It is a representation of Jackson's power that was split off from him
###### Abilities 
- The item serves as a Figurine of Wondrous Power. A _Figurine of Wondrous Power_ is a statuette of a beast small enough to fit in a pocket. If you use an action to speak the command word and throw the figurine to a point on the ground within 60 feet of you, the figurine becomes an Ancient Silver Dragon. See the *Monster Manual* for the creature's statistics. 
- As an action, the user can absorb the figurine. The user gains the following benefits:
	- You can tell whenever a creature is lying 
	- You can’t be charmed or frightened
	- As a bonus action on your turn you harness a part of Bahamut's power, adopting a transformation. You grow spectral dragon wings on your back, translucent scales on your body, golden draconic eyes, pointed teeth, and a spectral tail. You gain the following benefits for 10 minutes. You can conserve time by deactivating the transformation as a bonus action. You regain all uses after 24 hours.  
		- Your speed doubles and you gain a fly speed equal to your walk speed 
		- As a bonus action or replacing a attack action in the attack action, you can exhale a 20 foot cone of magical energy. Each creature in that area must make a Dexterity saving throw (DC = 8 + your Constitution or Spellcasting modifier + your proficiency bonus). On a failed save, the creature takes 4d10 damage of your choice between Fire, Lightning, Cold, Acid, or Thunder damage. On a successful save the creature takes half as much. 
		- You have blindsight up to 60 feet
		- Whenever you deal weapon or spell damage you deal additional Fire, Lightning, Cold, Acid, or Thunder damage (your choice) equal to your proficiency bonus
