The Vials of Random Brew are 3 magical potion vials used by [[Emily Sigiliph]]. 

###### Abilities 
- Once per day 3 random magical potions are filled within each different potion vial, each granting a unique effect. The DM gets to decide the effects or you can roll on the effects table for each vial
