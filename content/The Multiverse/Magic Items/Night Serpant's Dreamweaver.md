Night Serpant's Dreamweaver is a patron gift from Dendar, the Night Serpent
###### Abilities 
- The user can cast Phantasmal Force at will, without expending a spell slot. 
- You can cast Major Image, Fear, Phantasmal Killer, Seeming, Hallucinatory Terrain and Creation using Warlock Spell slots.
- You can cast Mental Prison Major Image (at 6th level), and Mirage Arcane once per long rest
- When you cast an illusion spell of 1st level or higher, you can choose one inanimate, nonmagical object that is part of the illusion and make that object real. You can do this on your turn as a bonus action while the spell is ongoing. The object remains real for 1 minute. For example, you can create an illusion of a bridge over a chasm and then make it real long enough for your allies to cross. Moreover, when you cast an illusion spell that has a duration of 1 minute or longer, you can use your action to change the nature of that illusion (using the spell's normal parameters for the illusion), provided that you can see the illusion.
	- At the end of a long rest, you can pick any one or pair of weapon, magical or non magical. You weave illusions together to create the weapon and make it real. The weapon disappears after 24 hours 
		- Rakdos RiteSwords (Short Swords 2x)
