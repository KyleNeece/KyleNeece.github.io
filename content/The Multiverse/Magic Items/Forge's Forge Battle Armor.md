Forge's Battle Armor is a set of magical plate armor 
- On your turn, you may expend a hit die (no action required) to increase your speed by 5 times the amount rolled and gain a fly speed equal to your walk speed until the end of your turn. 
- As an action you may expend a hit die to regain a number of hit points equal to the roll plus your constitution modifier 
- You have advantage on death saving throws
- As an action you may use the wrist mounted projectiles integrated within the suit. You are considered proficient in this weapon and on a hit it deals 2d6 force damage
- You may expend a hit die to gain darkvision for an additional 60 feet 
- You can read all writing 
- As a reaction if you would to be forcibly moved, you may reduce the amount of feet moved by 10 feet
