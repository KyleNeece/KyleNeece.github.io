Das Sternben is [[Otto]]'s Crossguard Energy Blade
###### Abilities 
Das Sterben is a Greatsword 
- Das Sterben deals 2d6 + Charisma modifier Radiant damage and has a +3 attack and damage modifier
	- As a reaction, when you are hit by an attack, you can use your reaction to gain a +2 to your AC, potentially turning the attack hit into a miss
	- As an action you can regain a warlock spell slot 
	- **(If in a contract with a patron)** You learn the Telekinesis Spell. You can cast it at will without expending a spell slot, but you can only manipulate objects when you cast it this way. You can spend a Warlock spell slot to use the full effect of the spell.
