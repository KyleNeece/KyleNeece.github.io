Fiendbane is [[Monty]]'s Handcannon
###### Abilities 
Fiendbane has a range 60/180, kick 12, loud, reload (6 shots, takes action or bonus action and need free hand to reload) 
- Fiendbane deals 1d8 + dexterity modifier piercing damage and has a +1 attack and damage modifier
	- The wielder is proficient with the weapon
	- You can inscribe a number of runes onto this weapon equal to your proficiency bonus, but can only benefit from the passive and invoked effects of one rune on the weapon at a time. As a bonus action you can can switch which rune the weapon is benefiting from.
