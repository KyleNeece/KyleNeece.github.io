Gift of the Serpent Mother is a magic Amulet given to Jackson Felwinter as an anniversary gift from Aria Hassan. 
###### Abilities 
- While you wear the amulet you know and can cast Eldritch Blast. You add your spellcasting modifier to the damage rolls 
- You can cast find familiar at will without expending a spellslot. You may only choose a snake in this way
- As an action you can create a magical construct of a item of your choice in an unoccupied space within 10 feet of you. The created item disappears after a number of hours equal to your proficiency bonus.
