Forge's Motor Hammer is Geneviève Forge's signature battle hammer 

###### Abilities 
- The hammer deals 2d6 + strength modifier bludgeoning damage 
- The hammer is a +3 weapon 
- As a bonus action, you can rev the Hammer for a rev counter by spending a hit die. You can have up to 3 rev counters. You can spend rev counters in the following ways 
	- 1 rev counter and an action: Deal an additional 2d6 damage. 
	- 2 rev counters and an action: You spin in a circle making an attack roll against everything within 5 feet of you. 
	- 3 rev counters and an action: Emit a 15 foot cone of flames originating from in front of you. Each target must make a dexterity saving throw equal to 8 + your constitution modifier + you proficiency bonus. On a fail all creatures within the 15 foot cone take 3d6 fire damage and on a success take half as much.
