Her Loyalty is a Modified Magic Firearm that was originally used by [[Freya]] and was later gifted away.
###### Abilities 
Her Loyalty is a Modified Magic Sidearm Firearm with 60/180, pocket and reload (6 shots, takes action or bonus action and need free hand to reload) 
- Her Loyalty deals 1d6 + dexterity modifier piercing damage and has a +1 to attack and damage rolls that scale with level (increases to +2 at Level 11, and +3 at Level 17)
	- (Deals less base damage due to custom integrated barrel silencers)
- When you make an attack roll from stealth using this weapon, you have advantage on the attack roll
	- When you are under the effects of invisible, when you make an attack roll with this weapon you automatically critically hit. Additionally you roll triple the damage dice instead of double when determining the critical hit damage.
