###### Abilities 
The Enchanted Blade is a +3 Longsword
- The +3 Weapon deals 1d8 + 2d6 Force Damage
	- **Modular Energy**: The sword's energy blade emits bright light in a 15-foot radius and dim light for an additional 15 feet. While the blade persists, you can use an action to expand or reduce its radius of bright and dim light by 5 feet each, to a maximum of 30 feet each or a minimum of 10 feet each.
	- **Justice Strike**: You can cast Steel Wind Strike in Blade mode 1x per long rest. Whenever you cast Steel Wind Strike, you can target the same target multiple times and add your weapon damage and effects once per separate target to the damage rolls. 
	- **Spell Storing**: This Weapon functions as a ring of Spell Storing with 5 charges.
	- **Energy Slash**: The blade also serves as a ranged weapon with a range of 20/60. You do not suffer disadvantage on attack rolls when using this blade within 5 feet of an enemy 
	- **Hydrangeas Healing**: This blade can heal allies. Whenever you attack an ally with this weapon, they may choose to be hit. On a hit, roll normal weapon damage. The ally hit regain hit points equal to half the attack's damage, rounded up.
