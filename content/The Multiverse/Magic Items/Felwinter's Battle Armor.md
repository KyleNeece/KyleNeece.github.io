Felwinter's Battle Armor is a battle armor passed through the Felwinter family 
###### Abilities 
- Your AC is equal to 14 + your dexterity modifier 
- Whenever you roll a 20 when on a weapon attack, you may cast a cantrip or a leveled spell no action required 
- Whenever you hit an enemy with a weapon or spell attack, you may expend a spell slot to deal your choice of Fire, Lightning, Cold, Acid, or Thunder damage in addition to the weapons damage. The extra damage is 2d6 for a 1st level spell slot. plus 1d6 for each spell slots higher than the 1st. 
- Whenever you kill an enemy you regain 1 hit die and can spend that hit die (no action required) to regain hit points equal to the roll
