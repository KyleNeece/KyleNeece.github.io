The grappling hook has a range of 30 feet. As an action you can make an attack roll with this weapon. On hit, you may choose one of the following features:
- **Offense.** You pull yourself towards the target to an unoccupied space within 5 feet of the target. As a part of same action, you may make one weapon or spell attack roll. 
- **Defense.**  You attempt to pull yourself towards the yourself to an unoccupied space within 5 feet of you. The target must make a DC15 strength saving throw. On a fail, the target is pulled towards you. The target can willingly fail the save.
