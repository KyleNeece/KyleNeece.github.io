The Wayseeker is a Spelljammer ship. It had the following qualities. Whenever you wish to fly it, you must attune to the Spelljammer and sit in its white plastic chair to drive it, which also grants inspiration. It also also the following qualities. 
- The interior has a standard furnished living space with a game table and multiple bedrooms 
- Pinecone sap holds together the exterior 
- A large metal rod serves as a battering Ram 
- A Bombard Mortar 
- 2x Fire Projectors cannons 
- A Roof spikes 
- The material is made out of magically enchanted and carved marble and granite stone 
- It sports mesa color schemes
