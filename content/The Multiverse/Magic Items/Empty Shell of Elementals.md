Empty Shell of Elementals is a magical shell
###### Abilities
The magical shell has 3 charges per day. The user can expend a charge as a bonus action to rub the shell on the designated weapon to cast and apply the effect of [Arcane Weapon](https://dnd5e.wikidot.com/spell:arcane-weapon) . All charges are regained after 24 hours.
