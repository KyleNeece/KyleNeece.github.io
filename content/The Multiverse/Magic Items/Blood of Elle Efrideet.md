The Blood of Elle Efrideet is an item used by [[Monkey (Alex)]], [[Aeris Arendor]], [[Otto]], [[Jaloop]], [[Monty]], [[Akanos]], [[Equilibrium]], [[Chuck]], and [[Gorlock (Rhulk)]]. The item contains the original blood of a Efrideet bloodline member, and provides buff effects
- The Blood has 1d4 Charges per week
	- The user can use the blood as an action to gain the effects of haste without lethargy. Only one member in the party can benefit from this at a time

**Holder**
- [[Otto]]
