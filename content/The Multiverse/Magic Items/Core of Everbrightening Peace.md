The Core of Everbrightening Peace is [[Yasu-2]]'s heart and signature magic item
###### Abilities 
- The core gives a +2 to your spell save DC and attack rolls
	- As a free action you can emit a bright light in a 15 foot radius and a dim light for an additional 15 feet. The light is sunlight and while this feature persists you can use an action to expand or reduce its radius of bright and dim light by 5 feet each, to a maximum of 30 feet each or a minimum of 10 feet each.
	- You gain a fly speed equal to your walk speed
	- You know the Firebolt and Mending Cantrips and they count as a cleric cantrips for you so long as you are attuned to this item 
	- You can use your Channel Divinity feature without expending one of the feature's uses. Once this property is used, it can't be used again until the next day.
	- Whenever you use your Warding Flare to protect someone that is being attacked, you can grant them temporary hit points equal to 2d6 + your wisdom modifier 
	- The Core has 1d4 charges, which recharges every 24 hours. Whenever you deal damage with a spell or channel divinity, you can spend a charge to activate a overdrive function to increase your damage potential. The spell or channel divinity ignores damage immunities and you roll each of the spell or channel divinity's damage dice with advantage.
