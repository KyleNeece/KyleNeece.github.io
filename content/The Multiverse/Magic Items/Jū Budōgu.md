- Weapon type: Longsword Sidearm Gunblade
	- Proficient while wielding it
	- +3 Magic item
	- Finesse property

- Features
	- At the beginning of your turn you may remove your proficiency bonus from attack rolls with this weapon until the end of the turn. When you do so choose one:
		- You gain a bonus to your AC equal to your proficiency bonus against the next attack roll made against you until the start of your next turn.
		- You gain a bonus to this weapons next damage roll equal to your proficiency bonus until the end of your turn.
	- When the user attunes to this weapon, they may choose an ability score (except constitution). The weapon uses the choose ability score for attack and damage rolls
	- Melee Damage Die: 1d8 
	- Ranged (40/120, loud, reload 6) Damage: Die 1d6
	- Dual Weapon: This weapon has 6 charges that recharge at dawn, you may spend 1 charge to use one of the following features:
		- Dual Threat: Whenever you make an attack roll on your turn, you may declare to make both a melee and ranged weapon attack on a target at the same time. If both attacks hit, you double the weapon damage dice. 
		- Dual Parry: Whenever you are hit by an attack roll, as a reaction you may make a melee and ranged weapon attack. If one of your attack's roll is higher then the opposing attack roll the attack misses and your attack hits. If both your attack rolls are higher, you double the weapon damage dice.
