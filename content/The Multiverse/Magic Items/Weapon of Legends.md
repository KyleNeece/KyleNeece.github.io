The Weapon of Legends is [[Emily Sigiliph]]'s magical eagle necklace that can transform into any weapon she chooses
###### Abilities 
Weapon of Legends is a +3 weapon 
- As a free action, the eagle necklace can transform into various legendary weapons. Each weapon has the properties of a immoveable rod and have the properties of a vorpal weapon
	- In Greatsword form it deals 2d6 damage 
	- In Bow form it deals 1d8 damage 
- In weapon form it also has the following features 
	- Immobilizing Impalement: Once per turn, you can make a weapon attack at disadvantage. On hit, the damage type changes to piercing and the target must make a athletics check DC25 or else be restrained. The target can repeat the check each turn.
	- Promised Victory: Once per week, Emily can channel all her might into the weapon, making a weapon attack so strong it ensures victory. As an action you can make one weapon attack against a creature you are in combat with. The creature must make a dexterity saving throw. The creature takes 40d6 force damage on a fail and half as much on a success
	- Targets have disadvantage on saving throws against your maneuvers and weapon actions 
	- You can use two weapon maneuvers instead of one whenever you expend a superiority die to use a maneuver
