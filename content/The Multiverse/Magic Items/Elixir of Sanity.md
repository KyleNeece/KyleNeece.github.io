The Elixir of Sanity is a **theoretical** brew that is the only supposeded cure tha can eliminate madness disease. 

## Steps to craft 

**Materials**
- Essence of One who accepts their end
- A Fragment of an Actor's Fable 
- A Mote of Darkness
- A Fragment of a Dead Star 
- A Broken Instrument 
- A Star's Broken Sponsor Document 
- 3 Silenced Everburning Candles 
- A Ritual Circle Written in Runic 

**Ritual**
- The Brew 
	- Mix each of the ingredients into a larger vial, and apply the maddened individual. Once this happens, immediately start the ritual starting with night 1 instructions. 
- Night 1: 
	- The afflicted is placed in the middle of the ritual circle and 3 candles for the 3 days the ritual is preformed, having the victim hold the instrument in their arms. The afflicted relives the event of contracting the disease through memory, the afflicted must survive the dream without going mad.
- Night 2: 
	- The afflicted must die, and sever their life force and return without cosmic madness claiming their body as host.
- Night 3: 
	- The afflicted has a choice. They may preform the following: 
		- Sacrifice one part of themselves. The art of equivalent exchange. This determines if the cure works or not 
		- Sacrifice a second part of themselves after the first. This may grant them new powers and abilities depending on the value of whats sacrificed and if it is of even more value then the 1st sacrifice, overriding and doubling equivalent exchange and giving permanent boons as part
			- This works by multiplying the negative outcome twice to gain a positive outcome
