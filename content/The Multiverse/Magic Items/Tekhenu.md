Tekhenu is [[Aeris Arendor]]'s Scimitar Bowblade
###### Abilities 
Tekhenu is a Scimitar Bowblade
- Tekhenu deals 1d6 + wisdom modifier damage and has a +1 attack and damage modifier
	- You can use your wisdom modifier when determining the attack and damage rolls with this weapon instead of your strength or dexterity modifier
- Tekhenu also has a matching quiver, which has the following properties 
	- The quiver has 1d4 charges. At the beginning of the day, roll on the Zodiac Sign Table. The quiver gains 1d4 of the special ammunition rolled on the table for the next 24 hours, where after it disappears.
