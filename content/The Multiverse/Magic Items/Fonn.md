Fonn is [[Jaloop]]'s magical shield that can also be played as a gong
###### Abilities 
Fonn gives +2 to ac and has a magical +1 to AC
- As a bonus action on your turn you can hit the shield with an object you are holding in your offhand, each creature within 30 feet that can hear it gains a bardic inspiration die. Once this feature is used you cannot use it again until a long rest
