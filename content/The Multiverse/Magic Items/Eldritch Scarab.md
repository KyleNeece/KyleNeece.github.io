The Eldritch Scarab is a sentient parasite that attached itself to [[Akanos]]. The user has to be bonded to the Scarab

	- The Scarab can consume different items (and sometimes parts of creatures, DM's discretion) in order to upgrade itself and grant the host new capabilities.

	- As an action you sprout spectral wings that take the appearance of your choice. While the wings are sprouted you can use them to fly up to 4 hours, all at once or in several shorter flights, each using a minimum of 1 minute from the duration, if you are flying when the duration expires, you descend at a rate of 30 feet per round until you land.
