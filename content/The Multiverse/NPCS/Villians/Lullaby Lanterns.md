##### Brief Summary 
Lullaby Lanterns are unique abberant creatures who's sole purpose is to sooth Meleena 
([[Meleena's Wetnurse]]) and to eliminate any threats that threaten the child. They originate from the [[City of Evernight]]
##### Appearance
 ![[Lullaby Lanterns.png]]
##### Brief History/Timeline   
The lanterns were created to sooth Meleena, so that it would not cry and would continue to have a peaceful sleep. Lullaby Lanterns are very stealthy. They attempt to incapacitate foes with their gaze, causing them to go mad, then they consume the targets soul.
