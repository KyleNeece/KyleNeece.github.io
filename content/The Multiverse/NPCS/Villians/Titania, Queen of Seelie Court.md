##### Brief Summary 
Queen Titania is the Summer Queen of the Seelie Court within the Feywild.
##### Appearance 
![[Queen Titania.png]]
##### Personality 
Queen Titania has a hard to classify personality. Some may see her as irresponsible and carefree, but in truth she is a very intelligent strategist both in court and politics, and the battlefield. She has a dry sense of humor and can be very pragmatic sometimes, as to her sometimes ends justify the means. While its hard to make her angry, if one were to rise her ire a most powerful wrath would be waiting for them. 
##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Origins/Adolescence
Nothing is known about Queen Titania's origins, only myths and legends
###### The Felwinter Saga
Queen Titania is worshiped like a Deity within the Feywild, and is considered one of the most mighty archfey. She serves as a patron for tons of Warlocks across the realms (similar to the Raven Queen). During the Felwinter Saga, she helped fight in the battle versus Tartarus.
