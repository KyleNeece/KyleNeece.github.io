##### Personality
The fool is a silly and unserious pain in the ass clown, who follows others around and watches them. They possess a flamboyant and clownish personality, maintaining cunning and intellectual prowess, but with a crazed personality. They constantly taunt, and even berate everyone, friend or foe when in this persona. Their power involves the Schrödinger's Cat paradox, where they can be everywhere, but no where at the same time. However, they do not seem to possess the harmful output to hurt others.
