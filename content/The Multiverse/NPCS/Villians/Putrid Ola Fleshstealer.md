##### Brief Summary
Putrid Ola Fleshstealer is a evil fey who enjoys the suffering of others.
> [!Warning]- Spoilers Here DO NOT CLICK
> She is in league with [[Orius Dryden]]

##### Personality
Putrid Ola Fleshstealer is sadistic and many consider her to be pure and twisted evil. She enjoys the suffering and sorrows of others. 

##### Brief History/Timeline

###### Campaign 2
Putrid Ola Fleshstealer in learning about an organization who wishes to reshape the world, joins them. She first invades Sigil, only to be repelled, which then she slinks into the Shadows. She then returns to the plane of Bant, along with a few of agents given to her, to attempt to assassinate [[Darrington Bant]]. Upon failure she then retreats again. She along with a [[Zutse Toxinfang]], subdue both Geppetto and Iris. The two have the both of them work on a machine that would be destruction incarnate and find the Sword of History. Upon intervention, Putrid Ola Fleshstealer is stopped and killed, and the machine disarmed.
