##### Brief Summary
Claymore was the build warforged assistant from infernal iron and magitech who was the primary flexible support assistant for The Fire Opal Devils.

![[Claymore.png]]

##### Origins
Claymore was built for the purposes of filling the spots that needed to be filled within The Fire Opal Devils company along with  [[Fwip]], [[Fedrett Benedict]], [[Elaran Polarshard]], [[Helter Skelter]], and [[Joshua Price]]. He is all around a good adventurer and a capable fighter. He has an extermination mode where it unlocks his limiters, making him a machine of war with no distinction of ally or enemy in a fight, just destruction.
