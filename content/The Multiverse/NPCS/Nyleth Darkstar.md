##### Brief History/Timeline

###### Origins/Adolescence

###### The Illithid Mage Arc and Betrayal
At some point in time during his universe crusade, Tartarus (see [[Azriel Darkstar]]) traveled to Dominaria as his very first plane, and didn't take it over until later. It was here he received training from [[Arangol]], but also at the same time, had a child he didn't even know about.

###### Declaration of War Arc and Panmensia Arc
Tartarus didn't dare go back near go back towards Dominaria, even at full strength, as they housed some of the most powerhouse of beings in the universe, so he sent his second in command instead, only to die to Karn. This was when Nyleth was still young and as such did not take part in the defense versus the invasion. 
Before the Panmnesia spell, Teferi phased out the entirety of the plane, and it came back into existence later in the timeline. 

###### Bridge between Cour 1 and Cour 2 
Before [[Jackson Felwinter]]'s capture in the Abyss, he journeyed to Dominara at the report (by Aria, Aria along with other hired adventurers went with him as well) of a large threat to the universe. At the time Nyleth was young, and apart of a orphanage that also served double as a religious church and community. When Jackson "assassinated" the pope during a service on a choir performance by [[Girlfriend name here]], chaos ensured and a battle within the church occurred. Nyleth sought battle with Jackson (Jackson was happy to see Nyleth, as he thought he had no more family after Azriel died). Upon Jackson being stabbed with Nyleth's [[Purple King]] and being completely fine, Nyleth realizes that Jackson only killed demons. Jackson pointing this out confuses Nyleth as Jackson manages to get away. 

The brother of [[Girlfriend name here]] then instructs Nyleth to pursue Jackson, which he then sets out to do so, he makes his way through Dominaria and finds different, unattended to hidden portals that are the cause of all the Demon invasions happening around the main city he was in. After running through, fighting and defeating a few higher level demons (only for them to flee), he comes across [[Aria Hassan]] who is disguised as member of the church. After some exposition, they go their separate ways. Nyleth then stumbles through a castle, and finds the head researcher (also [[Nicole Silvermetal]]'s father). He reveals that he created the portals in order to experiment with the demons. The researcher also reveals that using the power, the pope hopes to rule the world using a constructed god-like being that would be under the Pope's control. 

The researcher succeeds in killing Nyleth with a few of his demonized creatures he crafted, but Nyleth is revived and achieves a weak version of [[The Story Teller's Power]] through [[Azriel Darkstar]]'s old sword [[Heaven and Hell]], which had been held within a containment field inside the Researchers lab. Nyleth then dealt with the demons, and the Researcher fled. Nyleth then heads to the headquarters of the church, thinking that his [[Girlfriend name here]]'s brother knows more. After making it, Nyleth asks where Jackson is, and is told that he does not have the authority to demand answers from him. The brother and Nyleth then fight. As Nyleth wins, [[Girlfriend name here]] appears, interrupting the fight. Nyleth tries to explain himself, but then she is revealed to be held hostage by the pope, so that he could get closer to Nyleth. The brother and Nyleth then try to blitz the pope, only for him to leave. The two then go separate ways to track him. 

As Nyleth tries to track the pope, he runs into Jackson. He tries to ignore Jackson but Jackson makes the point that the sword was his brothers, and he wants it back. After a brief fight and Jackson revealing he was guiding him, (and Jackson realizing that he's Azriel's kid from when he was Tartarus) Jackson lets Nyleth keep the sword, showing their first step as allies. Nyleth then finds the giant god statue that the Pope had created. The pope asks if Nyleth will surrender and form the statues core together with [[Girlfriend name here]], but Nyleth refuses and attempts to save her. Falling into the Popes trap, the Pope takes Heaven and Hell. Suddenly, the brother appears and attempts to kill the Pope, only to be stabbed by Heaven and Hell. His body is caught by Jackson as Nyleth is absorbed into the heart of the statue. The brother asks Jackson for a final request, to save Dominaria, his sister and Nyleth.  

Much later (after Jackson backtracks Nyleths entire path during this event) Jackson weakness the statues exterior. Jackson thrusts Heaven and Hell into the core of the statue, awakening Nyleth and allowing him to escape the heart-like organ imprisoning him. After completing a number of trials within the statue, Nyleth finally confronts the pope. The pope, having achieved even greater levels of power with the machine, asks why Nyleth betrayed the Order. Nyleth answers that the Pope has done enough foul deeds to make Nyleth hate him. During their exchanges, the Pope also scoffs at Nyleth for being held back by love. The two then bear arms and fight

Nyleth finally defeats the Pope inside the statue. In a final bid for victory, Sanctus implores the statue or power, but his request remains unfulfilled. Nyleth saves [[Girlfriend name here]] and kills the Pope, and they escape the Savior. Although it seems that all is finally over, the Pope suddenly transforms into the form of the False statue, taking on the pope's appearance. Nyleth asks to fight in Jackson's stead, and Jackson acquiesces. Nyleth offers a quick prayer to God, during which he explains how he's come to terms with his runic power, and destroys the False statue and the Pope once and for all by using it.

Nyleth meets up with Jackson, with whom he exchanges thanks and friendship. With Jackson about to leave, Nyleth tries to return Heaven and Hell back to him, but he refuses to take it, saying it's now entrusted to Nyleth. In their final moments together, Nyleth asks Jackson if they'll meet again, but Jackson just vaguely waves in answer.

###### Campaign 2
At some point, by orders of his uncle via a letter, Nyleth was instructed to trace the murders of a few members of the Dryden clan (one of the four naturally powerful human clans, the others being Felwinter, Efrideet, and Forge). Jackson gave him Aeris's information if he wanted help on the case, and while Nyleth didn't really feel the need from a combat perspective, he thought his wisdom, experience, and another brain would be helpful. He sought out Aeris, and the two began their investigations.
