##### Brief Summary
Darrington Bant is the current King of the region Bant located on the Alara plane

##### Appearance
![[Darrington Bant.png]]

##### Campaign 2
Upon the party making it to Bant, [[Gabe (Gortat) and Andy the Stork]] sought audience with him to learn more about the Sword of History. King Bant lead the party and their companions around his castle to the Japanese Tea Gardens located inside. He explained to them the genocide of the four noble human clans, as well as the potential whereabouts of the Sword of History, and gave Andy cryptic info regarding the relocation of his Kingdom and their relation to the Sword. He then hired them to be bodyguards during his party which celebrates his anniversary of him being King. During the party, the [[Putrid Ola Fleshstealer]] attempted at his life, but he was able to survive. 

##### Abilities
Darrington is a skilled ruler and a solid fighter due to his high level of peak fitness
