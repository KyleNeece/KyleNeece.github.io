##### Brief Summary
Elaran Polarshard is a High Elf who is the founder and executive of the Fire Opal Devils. 

##### Appearance
![[Elaran Polarshard.png]]

##### Origins
Being once a sargent of the Abyss's forces, Elaran Polarshard encountered [[Joshua Price]] numerous times. After being defeated by his company multiple time, Elaran finally decided to turn to the Devil's side, seeing the cyst of the universe for what it was. He then became the lead and founder of the company the Fire Opal Devils. He was then placed in the company along with [[Fwip]], [[Fedrett Benedict]], [[Elaran Polarshard]], [[Helter Skelter]], and [[Claymore]]. These were the main members, along with many other high capability soldiers. After a raid in the abyss went south, Fwip defected, as despite living the encounter Joshua fled, essentially disbanding the team. He then exercised his spoils and riches from the raids in hell, and went to the Feywild within [[Thieves Metropolis]].
