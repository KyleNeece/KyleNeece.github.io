A peacekeeper is a soldier of the peacekeeper force, who are the invisible protectors of the multiverse. The [[Peacekeeper Main Base of Operations]] is located on [[Sigil]]
###### General Background
After the [[Sigil Academy, Rising Heroes Arc 6 - Panmnesia Arc]], [[Jackson Felwinter]], who's simulacrum was destroyed by [[Arangol]]'s avatar, established the Peacekeeper military force. Arangol took control of the force in secret, creating a militarized army that would end up doing his bidding and help him gain power over different planes. This was to help with his ultimate plan of defeating [[The Omnipotent One]].
