Hughie Goldsmith is a blacksmith originating from Ebberon 

##### Brief Summary
Hughie Goldsmith grew up on Ebberon as a all purpose arms-smith. He has good skill in crafting weapons, armor, and sometimes other magical items. He found his way to the Dryden estate because of his good smithing and crafting skills, earning him and spot in the estate. He views his purpose as crafting the ultimate item that can kill a god.

##### Campaign 2

Hughie began working in the Dryden estate as the main smith, and saw the race to find the Sword of History as a opportunity to craft the ultimate object to kill a god like being. Upon [[Quoric Neckelo]] giving him Jackson Felwinters old blade ([[True Magi's Blade]]), he saw opportunity to help the adventuring party repair it, though knows it'll never be the same artifact as it once was due to Jackson discarding it after [[The Istoria Rytsarey]] invasion and the [[4.4_Act 3 - Omnipresent War Arc]].
