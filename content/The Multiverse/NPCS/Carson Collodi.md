Carson Collodi (given the nickname Bebop) is a Warforged who originates from the plane of Ebberon

##### Personality
The Unnamed One was one of the first original prototypes for the making Machine's that were alive and autonomous. He possesses no personality traits, but is willing to learn what emotions and states of beings are. He hopes to be able to become more human and do good and adventures to learn more as such. 

##### Brief History/Timeline

###### Origins/Adolescence
The Unnamed One originated from Ebberon as one of the first prototypes of Machines that were alive and autonomous, created by [[Giuseppe Geppetto]] who perfected [[Jotor Sparklefen]]'s creation of machine sentient life. However, the project was shut down, as at the time technology was not advanced enough for others to believe that it was to be deemed safe for such machines to have a mind of their own. (This assumption was true, as the motive of these machines were to eventually serve in an dominant war machine army). As such, he turned to junk and scrap, and was shut off.
