###### Origins
The Infinite Reader was one of the first born in the multiverse and was one of the creators along with [[The Omnipotent One]]. He helps manifest the stories of Fantasy into reality. One day he left the library, like the Omnipotent One, causing a slow shift and imbalance that would progress millions of years
