##### Brief Summary
Iris Mindstring is a Engineer who's Warforged design was the predecessor to [[Geppetto]] but the evolution of [[Jotor Sparklefen]]'s model.

##### Personality
Iris is very moldable, and conflicted person. While her intelligence, experiments, and rations are sometimes inhumane, she practices them for hoping to achieve humane objectives. She is deeply depressed and will do anything to achieve her life long goals, but hates herself when she is about to or crosses a line. Her intentions are good but is often within tough or shitty happenings.

##### Brief History/Timeline

###### Origins
Iris is a master puppeteer and was revolutionary in her field of developing Warforged. Growing up, she looked up to a woman named Katherine, whom she saw as a mother figure. After Katherine passed, Iris came up with the idea to put her magical cosmic essence and soul into a maid Warforged puppet so that she could live on. The Warforged puppet later displayed signs of humanity by saving a baby from falling from its crib. However, when [[Aria Hassan]] learned of this, they interrogated Katherine and tore her apart, as Iris had found a way of harnessing the Magical Weave of the multiverse in a similar way to how Aria is able to harness Dendar's power by herself with her Nightmare summons. Iris's experiments left her at odds with many pro-law peoples.  

###### Campaign 2
Iris continued to hold grief about Katherine and others who have passed away, she continuously attempts to try and find the way to resurrect her loved ones and those she cared about from the dead in a natural way. She reached a point in her research where she can transfer a essence of a sentient being into a Warforged puppet body, but not to where they can become natural again. When the [[Putrid Ola Fleshstealer]] promised Iris that one of her colleagues could bring Katherine back, she aligned herself with the hag on the condition that no one were to be harmed.

##### Abilities
