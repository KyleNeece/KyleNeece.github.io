##### Brief Summary
Hoo Dini is a Owlin Wizard and originates from Waterdeep

##### Personality
Hoo Dini is a Wise, Intelligent, kind, and well spoken fellow, but decides to be silly and whimsical. He is very laid back and relaxed, and has a great love for mysteries. He is also very good at escaping any situation or binding he finds himself in, comparable to even the greatest of escape artists. He loves investigating the unknown, and holds eccentric habits like scribbling cryptic notes or muttering to himself in different incantations. 

##### Brief History/Timeline

###### Origins/Adolescence
Hoo Dini's origins are unknown, and he likes to tease about it and keep them that way. He makes references sometimes about old lore and stories, which makes many question if he is just a very smart and intelligent person, or if he has been around for a very very long time. He serves as an advisor for his peers, and is always available to learn more information for, for a price however. Some payments include strange items or even some gold pieces would work as well.
