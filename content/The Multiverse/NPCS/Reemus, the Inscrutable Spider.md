##### Brief History/Timeline   
Reemus the Inscrutable Spider is one of the three Great Old Ones who was the offspring of Dox Vulethix (Outer God Great Old One), and [[Maren, Queen of Evernight]]. Reemus the Inscrutable Spider lives in a endless lake, and its existence is the only thing holding the Bloodmoon and reality of Evernight back from the Eldritch horrors.
