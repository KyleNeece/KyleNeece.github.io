##### Brief Summary
Veronica Vileblood is a Dhampir Echo Knight Fighter originating from Evernight

##### Appearance
![[Veronica Vileblood.png]]

##### Personality
Veronica is upbeat, joyful, and carefree who enjoys being around other people. At the same time in battle, she is fierce, rough, and tough, and is considered to be a psycho in battle, and has partially inherited the powerful features of a full vampire. When she woke up, she didn't remember almost anything from her past, only that she was lost and looking for her master again, [[Ludwig Van Scythe]]. While she was in tutelage under Ludwig, she did not inherit his smarter qualities, as sometimes when it comes to common sense she falls flat. She hopes to be able to become as great as Ludwig and wishes to explore the world and learn how to fit into society to live a better life herself. 

##### Brief History/Timeline

###### Origins/Adolescence
Veronica originally grew up homeless, and was abandoned as a baby when her mother and father hid her away after The Draculas of Cainhurst wiped out the Vilebloods, due to the prejudice judgement that they were a threat (Oddly enough, it was the evil version of [[Otto]] that commenced this genocide). This occurred because the Draculas didn't want another powerful house of Vampires to exist. 
Ludwig found her, and brought her to an orphanage away from Evernight, as it was no place for a child to grow up. Veronica had a harder childhood and suffered a sense of un-belonging, not fitting it. Ludwig would always come by the orphanage, and would train her and be her only friend. He could never bring himself to adopt her however, as at the time he had his own priorities and no time to raise a child. By her later teenage years, she lived on her own.

###### The Felwinter Saga
Veronica was one of Ludwigs trainees during his younger years of life. However, when Ludwig left her, she sought to find him once again. On her journey, she passed away in an unknown fashion, becoming only bones for the longest time. 

###### Campaign 2
Veronica was revived from a few drops of spilled blood when [[Putrid Ola Fleshstealer]]'s and a few forces of unknown descent invaded [[Sigil]] during the yearly fair. Upon her revival, she ran into the party, and became a traveling companion with them. Because of this, she found her way to the Dryden estate, and now searches for her old teacher, [[Ludwig Van Scythe]], either through adventure, or the Sword of History.
