##### Brief Summary 
Sprocket McGob is a Goblin Merchant and S-Rank [[Peacekeeper]]
##### Appearance 
![[Sprocket McGob.png]]
##### Personality 
Sprocket loves and breathes biz. Sprocket is also a swindler, always trying to negotiate the best prices. Sprocket takes pride in his ability to sell and create items along with Jotor Sparklefen with ease, providing the best crafts and items in the multiverse. Sprocket is very creative, being an artisan of many talents and spends his days in [[Sigil]] selling items, which is what he loves to do.
##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Origins
Sprocket actually grew up on sigil, and came from a family of high wealth that ran the best goods shop. He eventually inherited his families shop, and changed the name, becoming the main shop that an adventurer or Peacekeeper goes to for almost anything. His day changed when he finally decided to go out on his first and last adventure. On this adventure, he found a container that could literally create anything he wished for, but only for the purposes of selling or giving to others. After claiming such an item, Sprocket had infinite possibilities, and his shop became a huge success. 
###### Recruitment
[[Jackson Felwinter]] gave him the S-Rank title and made him co-run the magic item system that [[Jotor Sparklefen]] also co-runs with him, making sure the peacekeepers get the best tech and items in the multiverse.
