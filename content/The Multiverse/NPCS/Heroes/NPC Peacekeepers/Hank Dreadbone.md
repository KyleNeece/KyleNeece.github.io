##### Brief Summary 
Hank Dreadbone is a Skeleton Curse of Possession Accursed originating from the Shadowfell and S-Rank [[Peacekeeper]]
##### Appearance 
![[Hank Dreadbone.png]]
![[Ire.png]]
##### Personality 
Hank, despite the name, is actually laidback, eccentric, and a jokester. Despite his appearance as a skeleton, he is a pretty likable guy and people enjoy being around him, even if the vibe is a little strange because he's a talking skeleton. In combat, he uses dark curse magic to his advantage.
##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Adolescence 
Not much is known about Hank's adolescence, all that is known is that he died at a very young age and was magically resurrected by his cursed spirit, [[Ire.png|Ire|]], for unknown reasons. 
###### Twin Prodigies Arc
Hank Dreadbone was one of the main exchange students that came for a semester abroad at [[Peacekeeper Academy]], along with the rest of the party ([[Johnny Sigiliph]], [[Tanith Iceburn]], [[Malatar Wütend]], [[Ryū Hoshi]], and [[Lillith Ashford]]) that would become rivals with [[Jackson Felwinter]]'s crew ([[Azriel Darkstar]], [[Aria Hassan]], [[Olivia Birdsong]], [[Emily Sigiliph]], and Yasu-2). Hank helped [[Yasu-2]] disperse the party from their pointless rivalry banter, as he found it a drag like Yasu-2 did.
###### House Tournament Arc
Hank Dreadbone, like the rest of Jackson's and Johnny's group, finished very high in most of the events. He however, lost to Emily Sigiliph, as her raw fighting prowess overtook his curse and antimagic abilities.
###### The Illithid Mage Arc
During [[Arangol]]'s first invasion on [[Sigil]], Hank was one of the key students that drove off the invasion. He was able to combat against the high ranking mages within Arangol's force using his Antimagic magical curse techniques. After the invasion, Hank began continue to embrace his curse even more and took the time to grow it.
###### Betrayal Arc
When Tartarus invaded, Hank immediately attempted to seek him out because Hank figured that his skillset was the perfect counter to Tartarus. However, he figured this and as such sent one of his best agents against Hank. When he faced off with Tartarus's agents, he was defeated by a higher level curse magic then his own.
###### Declaration of War Arc
Hank gathered the dead corpses of evil beings and shared his curse with them, reanimating and brining them to the final battle. He was one of the main special forces in battle tasked with clearing out the mages in Tartarus's force.
