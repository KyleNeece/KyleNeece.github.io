##### Brief Summary 
Tanith Iceburn is a Dragonborn Sorcerer originating from Faerun and is a S-Rank [[Peacekeeper]]
##### Appearance 
![[Tanith Iceburn.png]]
##### Personality 
Tanith is boisterous and talkative. She is interested in other cultures and enjoys learning about people's day to day lives. She shows a more fiery side when she is practicing something that she enjoys, and a more cooler side when she's with others and the vibe is less energy based. In combat, she typically resorts to massive magical explosions from her spells and blowing everything up. 
##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Adolescence
Tanith had two Dragons as parents, however both of them were shapeshifted into humans at the time of her "creation". She was then raised among dragons, honing her sorcerer magic efficiently. Once she grew up, Tanith was then determined and encouraged to attend the peacekeeper academy, where she could hone her craft and become a great adventurer. 
###### Twin Prodigies Arc
Tanith Iceburn was one of the main exchange students that came for a semester abroad at Peacekeeper academy, along with the rest of the party ([[Johnny Sigiliph]], [[Malatar Wütend]], [[Ryū Hoshi]], [[Hank Dreadbone]], and [[Tanith Iceburn]]) that would become rivals with [[Jackson Felwinter]]'s crew ([[Azriel Darkstar]], [[Olivia Birdsong]], [[Emily Sigiliph]], [[Yasu-2]], and [[Aria Hassan]]. Tanith had a small conflict with Aria, which was broken up by Yasu and Hank. 
###### House Tournament Arc
Tanith Iceburn, like the rest of Jackson's and Johnny's group, finished very high in most of the events. She however, lost to Yasu-2, as his magic was superior to hers due to his support from Iallanis
###### The Illithid Mage Arc
During [[Arangol]]'s first invasion, Tanith was one of the key students that drove off the invasion. She made quick work for the many hordes of Necro and Host Walkers with her destructive magic 
###### Betrayal Arc
When Tartarus invaded, Tanith was too busy defeating the hordes of monsters to engage with Tartarus or any high ranking general.
###### Declaration of War Arc
Tanith gathered an army of Dragons and Drangonborn warriors to help fight in the big battle to protect [[Sigil]] 
###### Panmnesia Arc

Tanith Iceburn was auto admitted into the peacekeeper force due to her finesse, support and fighting capabilities and was given S-Rank
