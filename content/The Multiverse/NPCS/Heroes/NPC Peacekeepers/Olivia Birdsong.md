##### Brief Summary 
Olivia Birdsong is a Elf bard originating from the Sword Coast and is a S-Rank [[Peacekeeper]]
##### Appearance 
![[Olivia Birdsong.png]]
##### Personality 
Olivia is charismatic, empathetic and kind, but troubled at the same time. She has difficulty opening up to others, tends to avoid emotional conflict by keeping others at arm distance away. Olivia usually attempts to never see combat, as she tries to use her charm to stop conflict before it even starts. If her charismatic words fail however and a fight does break out, she suffers from PTSD at the sight of those who are close to her becoming hurt, hindering her abillity to defend herself. Olivia has a beautiful voice and whistle, sounding like a chorus of birdsong when preformed. 
##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Adolescence
Olivia was born on shoreline of the Sword Coast and grew up with a happy family. Her mother was a well renowned Human Bard and her father was a strong yet compassionate Elf Warrior. However, tragedy struck. When the Birdsong family was on a ship, a band of Pirates raided, ultimately killing everyone aboard except Olivia. The pirates decided to keep Olivia against her will, using her for whatever they desired from other people or herself. During this time, Olivia learned the art of deception and trickery, ultimately escaping the pirate band and fleeing. She relied on deception and trickery to survive, but was often conflicted when using such tactics because she hated dishonesty. 
###### Twin Prodigies Arc
Olivia, having made enough coin through unwanted prostitution and thievery, finally scraped up enough money to enter the peacekeeper academy to get an education and attempt to get over her PTSD by starting a new life. At the house sorting, Olivia met [[Azriel Darkstar]], who gave her comfort due to the nature of his character, causing her to sit next to him and [[Jackson Felwinter]]. She made conversation with Azriel, instantly developing a starting attraction toward him due to his powerful yet compassionate characteristics. She was sorted into House Beacon. She later on met [[Aria Hassan]] and [[Yasu-2]], and the group became good friends. 
###### House Tournament Arc
During the House Tournament Arc, Olivia finished number 1 in the Social Networking challenge due to her excellent persuasive skills
In the Gem Hunt Challenge, Olivia finished middling, she did this by charming other contestants in order to find a gem and seize it for herself
In the Battle-Madness Challenge, Olivia lost to her first round matchup to [[Emily Sigiliph]], as Emily's indomitable will was too powerful against anything she could create.
Olivia was in the stands when [[Arangol]] and his forces invaded, using her persuasive words and assisting [[Twitch]] in turning the disoriented peacekeepers into a capable force that preformed incredible teamwork. This temporarily turned the tide of the invasion and repel it back. However, Arangol saw this and attempted to target her with his forces. Olivia was wounded, and almost died, but Azriel came to her aid and helped heal her wounds. Olivia upon waking up saw the terrible sight of Azriel unleashing his uncontrollable power, and cowered in fear seeing a normally loving Nephilim filled with anger.
###### The Illithid Mage Arc
Azriel eventually apologized to Olivia for scaring her with his immense dark energy. Olivia accepted it and told him not to worry.
Olivia began to notice a change in Azriel's mental state. She would ask if he was feeling ok. Azriel, who wanted her to not worry about him continued to say he was ok, until one night, he disappeared from the academy. Olivia asked around [[Sigil]], but could not find Azriel.
###### Betrayal Arc
Azriel eventually apologized to Olivia for scaring her and she accepted. However, she noticed a change in Azriel's mental state. Olivia would ask if Azriel was feeling ok. Azriel, who wanted her to not worry about him continued to say he was ok, until one night, he disappeared from the academy. Olivia asked and asked around [[Sigil]], but no one knew where Azriel had gone to.
It was when Tartarus invaded, that Olivia's heart shattered. Though she fought for Sigil in the surprise attack launched by Arangol's forces, she cried and despaired at the sight of Azriel, now Tartarus Enslaver of Worlds, destroying everything. 
After the battle, Olivia's mental state was too shattered to the point where she needed to go to a mental facility in order to attempt to cure her shattered state, leaving a conflicted Jackson Felwinter on whether to kill Azriel or try to talk sense into him 
###### Panmnesia Arc
 > [!Warning]- Spoilers Ahead
 > Per request of Jackson Felwinter, Olivia's trauma, mind and heart were fixed, with the downside of the Panmnesia spell
