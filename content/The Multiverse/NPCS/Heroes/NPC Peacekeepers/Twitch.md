##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Adolescence
Twitch grew up in southern Faerun in a nomadic clan of Kenku warriors. The members of the tribe hailed from different backgrounds, but they all shared in common a military background and lineage. The clan were warriors for hire called the Watchful Birds. They would only serve those of good values and traits. Twitch at a young age became the leading strategist in battle, directing his troops to victory in almost every battle. Twitch decided to leave the tribe, taking his talents to [[Sigil]] to challenge his intelligent tactical mind.
###### Twin Prodigies Arc
Twitch had already known [[Jackson Felwinter]] before his time at the academy due to meeting him on Sigil when he was little. The two however moved in different groups, but were still very close friends regardless
###### House Tournament Arc
Twitch did well in the house tournament, finishing high in all events. He lost in the combat tournament versus [[Emily Sigiliph]], who's combat prowess was greater then his. The incredible choreography of pure fighting skill (no magic or gimmicks) between the two made it the most entertaining fight in the tournament
###### The Illithid Mage Arc
Twitch was one of the first to act during the [[Arangol]] invasion. Taking quick command and controlling the battlefield. Even Arangol mentioned that his intelligence and strategy rivaled his, giving him praise for his high capabillity
###### Betrayal Arc
During [[Azriel Darkstar]]'s slow descent into evil due to Arangol's influence, Twitch returned to his homeland to gather all the warriors from his old tribe together. He told them the situation and to stand by for a potential attack that he hypothesized. 
Surely enough, Azriel attacked, and Twitch and his elite forces made it back just in time in order to turn the tide of battle, minimizing casualties.
###### Declaration of War Arc
Twitch gathered legendary warriors far and wide, and they all agreed to temporarily be inducted into his court of heroes. Twitch amassed one of the greatest fighting forces the multiverse has seen simply from his tactics, his ability to network with high ranking warriors and combining with Emily Sigiliph's forces were the bulk of the special forces branch of the amassed forces in the battle on Sigil
