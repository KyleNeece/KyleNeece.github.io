##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Early Ages
Monty originated from a pocket dimension located in an unknown world, most likely a universe or two over from universe 1. He studied runic magic with a focus on Abysal runes while keeping his physical expression style similar to that of Jotun.
 > [!Warning]- Spoilers Here Click at Own Risk 
 > 
 > One day, the  [[Sisters of Fallen Sanctity|Sisters of Fallen Sanctity]], who were previously two separate rune keepers, where actually trying to defend Monty's planet, instead of destroying it like Monty had perceived. At this moment, The Infinite Reader, the leader of [[The Istoria Rytsarey|The Istoria Rytsarey]], a group of powerful Runekeepers, activated his Story Retainment, absorbing all life on the planet as well as all Runekeeper's powers. However, Monty was unaffected and his ignorance led him to believe that his planet was destroyed be a demonic entity, who he seeks to hunt down.
 
###### The Peacekeeper Trials Arc
Monty participated in the peacekeeper trials in order to become enlisted within the force. He and 7 others were assigned to [[Ludwig Van Scythe]], who oversaw their trial. Completing the trials, Monty was assigned the D letter peacekeeper rank, and would eventually work his way up into the higher tiers. He also however, inherited the soul of [[Azriel Darkstar|Tartarus]], causing Ludwig to task them with resurrecting him.

###### The Evernight Hunt Arc
Monty was apart of the group that was assigned to the Shadowfell with Ludwig and [[Lady Gabriella Alucard II]], for the purposes of claiming land and building influence among the plane. The party journey throughout the wilds of the shadowfell before making it to the [[City of Evernight]]. They were able to take out one of [[Arangol]]'s researchers, the [[Landlord]], the keeper of the magical mansion. After defeating him and destorying the [[Necrowalkers]] and [[Hostwalkers]] laboratory, turning the estate into the peacekeeper force, the party continued to push forward, eventually making it to a graveyard, where one of [[Nathan Murgul]]'s estates is located. The party pushed forward into the city after a brief meeting with Nathan, learning more about the area and smaller details about the city. Monty and the party entered the city, after getting a taste of what true madness felt like after smelling some of the white orchids that they picked from the field leading to the bridge. After a few strange dreams, the party made it into the city, and heard church bells ring as the Evernight Hunt began. After dispatching a few monsters, a profane soul hunter lead the group back to their Workshop to meet [[Gerald, The First Hunter]], who would induct them into their hunter ranks to fight off the beasts of the night. 

Monty and the party continued on through the City of Evernight, they met a little girl by the name of [[Anna Efrideet]], who was home alone with her sister due to her mother going out to find their father. After, the eventually encountered their first problematic beast, Ben Efrideet, who transformed into a beast due to the blood curse and killed his wife. After a hard fight, the part was able to kill the frenzied and transformed profane soul hunter. The party made their way back to the house, finding a crying Anna who killed her sister as she was transforming. Taking the girl with them, Monty and the party made it to the District of Healing. Upon arrival to the Healing Church, the party meet [[Alder]], a Devotion Paladin of Selune. Alder allowed for the party to stay within the church, as it was a safe place where people could rest and stay. 

The party continued on deeper into the city. Eventually making it to a Magical Wendigo, who was guarding a magical skull. After making work of the Wendigo, the party touched the magic skull. It held more of [[Otto]]'s memories, and it revealed to the group that Otto was defeated by [[Jackson Felwinter]]. Upon this realization, Alucard II and Alder meet them with hostility, evaluating whether or not Otto would be a threat to the force. Otto choose to be civil, and the party then continued on through the City of Evernight, eventually making it to Castle Cainhurst. They party met the newest ruler, [[Ariana Draculina]]. They also found out that Otto was the third ruler, and the one before him was Alucard, but the time gap between the rulings was extensive. 

After their depature from Castle Cainhurst, the party continued through the city, eventually making it to a small sub district within the City. the party met [[Anna Efrideet]]'s aunt, Elle, and the party helped in stoping the [[Shadow of Evernight]] from continuing to terrorize the town. Upon his defeat, Tartarus became stronger, and learned the Abberant Rune Language. The party then made it to a lake, where [[Reemus, the Inscrutable Spider]] inhabited. Upon it's defeat, the party triggered the blood moon and lifted a lot of the obscuring magic that [[Sai Mona]] had cast. The party then made their way through the unseen district, and defeated the [[Trapped Souls of Evernight]]. A portal then opened leading to the nightmare of Sai Mona, to which the players entered. The party made a long and difficult journey to the top of a tower, where [[Meleena's Wetnurse]] was located and a projection of [[Maren, Queen of Evernight]] stood in dispair. Uponn the Wetnurses defeat and Maren's lifted grief, the party then made it back to the workshop. 

At the workshop Gerald wanted to free them of the hunt, but before he could do so Ludwig interfered, after a back and fourth of dialogue, the party found that Gerald was being controlled by Sai Mona unwillingly and was the host of the dream. Upon the party and Ludwig defeating Gerald, [[Sai Mona]] descended, and the party did battle. [[Jaloop]], Chuck, and [[Ludwig Van Scythe]] perished, but in Ludwigs last words he gave the party a choice: two scrolls of pure resurrection. Ludwig then passed, and Jaloop and Chuck were saved. Jaloop absorbed Sai Mona's essence, and the hunt came to an end. Alder took Anna Efrideet and the Healing Church Paladins to Sigil, and the party completed their mission. 

###### Ghost Treason Arc
A few months after the hunt, Monty studied Runic Magic on his own and worked to craft new equipment. After a short reunion, Monty and the party went to the Multiversal Tavern, where they relaxed and indulged in their services. After a plethora of interesting events, the party then got ready for Ludwig's funeral. After the service, [[Maui Rockheart]] gave them Ludwigs key to his office. In the office, the party found many things, but the most important being the recipe and requirements for the Ritual of Soul Reincarnation. The party was also assigned a new regiment leader, [[Emily Sigiliph]]. The party then ventured to the Feywild with her, as she said that she knew that Ludwig was trying to resurrect [[Azriel Darkstar]], and that she was willing to pick up his work where he left it off. Upon arriving to the Feywild, she entrapped the party within a barrier, and revealed that she had been working for [[Arangol]]. After Monty nearly killing everyone from an explosion (though it was later revealed that Emily didn't actually die...), he was incapacitated by [[Aeris Dad]], and [[Aeris Mom]]. They took them to a base of operations where [[Emily Sigiliph]], [[Yasu-2]], and [[Olivia Birdsong]] additionally where awaiting their waking as well. Emily then explained that she had took part-time leave from the peacekeeper force to work for Arangol as a mercenary so that she could track his movement and plans better. Because of her temporary leave and the completing of her contract, Emily is currently in freelance, and her along with the other allies agreed to help the party with their next journey through the Feywild. 

Emily suggested the party set out within the Feywild in order to find the Flower of Souls Persona and the Quantum Glyph of Resurrection. Upon the party leaving the sanctuary/citadel, Jaloop and Monty began to feel the symptoms caused from having multiple soul essences attached to one body. After this event, the party received a mysterious call from a person by the name of [[Daliah]]. They received it directly from their Peacekeeeper communication devices. Daliah instructed them to make their way to [[Thieves Metropolis]] (ran by [[Kindrack Diablo]]) because she and [[Titania, Queen of Seelie Court]], were aboard *The Arrowing*, the Queen's ship. She also reveals that she knows about their situation and that she is experiencing a sickness similar to that of Jaloop and Monty and knows about the condition. The party upon making it to the city make contact with Daliah. However, she appears in soul form, similar to that of [[Azriel Darkstar|Tartarus]]. It is revealed that she can even see and interact with Tartarus, and Jaloop and Monty black out, only for Daliah to have muted Tartarus from communication with the party. 

The party sneaks their way through the Thieves Metropolis through the outer walls, eventually coming to a huge vantage point. The party sees the ship, and a projectile hit it, causing it to catch fire and crash within the city. The party rushes through the Metropolis, fighting through Kindrack Diablo's soldiers to make it to the ship. Upon getting to it, they find the Queen, who lost her lesser deity powers temporarily due to the missile shot at it, and Daliah missing. The party fights and sneaks their way through waves of soldiers and even takes out one of Kindracks Spyder Tanks. Upon making it to a run down building, the party and the Queen rest for the evening. 

In the morning, the Queen tasks the party with activating a sleeper agent. Moreover, the party swears an oath to serve the Seelie Court, one of two primary political factions within the Feywild. The party decides to send Jaloop to talk with the sleeper agent. He talks through a old communication device, and tells Jaloop to meet him at the old Siegeball courts. The rest of the party do their own things. Otto gambles, Monty searches for Runes, Aeris and Monkey (Alex) go to the underground fighting pits, where they meet a mysterious man. After the party leaves for their solo activities, [[Quinn Murgul]] contacts them, telling them that Nathan his brother is acquaintances with them, and that he has granted them passess in and out of the city, and that he wants to meet them and do business with them. 

Upon Jaloops arrival, a mysterious man meets Jaloop and questions him. He holds a Handcrossbow to his gut. Jaloop tells him the situation and hand, and the man tells him to meet with him later in the evening along with the rest of his party members. In the evening, the party meets with the agent, [[Mark Reinford]]. Mark and the party move toward a scavengers den, where they sneak it and find a old Divination Wizard contact of Mark's, who locked himself in a room when the scavs took over so that the scavs wouldn't find him. Upon finding the Wizard and attempting to have him make contact with Daliah, he says that he's been made, and his head explodes. Mark and the party then stealthily escape, and look for another lead. 

Mark then leads the party to a bar, where a disguised black ops agent, [[Freya]], that Mark was once comrades with is in hiding. When Mark and the party meet her, she is pissed at Mark, due to the fact that Mark did not tell her about his cover story regarding his death. The party is able to convince Freya to be activated as an agent once again, and the party looks for leads. The party makes their way to Quinn, who gives them three jobs for the reward of lots of gold and info on Kindrack Diablo. The party split to finish these missions, Finding the Glyph of Quantum Resurrection on one of them. After their completion, Quinn gives them info on a party which will have celebrities from around the universe. The Mark and Freya make a plan, infiltrating the party to extract Daliah from Kindrack Diablo. 

The day after in the evening, the party executes the plan. They make their way through the flood tunnels of the building and through a scaffolding tower, taking out guards along the way. The party finally make it to the party, and put on disguises to blend in. The party and Mark find Daliah, but only for Daliah to tell them that Kindrack has the magical celestial orb that was needed for Jaloop, Monty, and Daliah's cure. This causes Mark to guess that Daliah was the one who made the attack on Queen Titania's ship. The party, Mark, Freya and Daliah then come up with another plan. The party plays blackjack with a pair of twins in order to get their behavioral profiles, so that they can be the ones to attend the meeting with Kindrack and the Orb. The party is able to get the profiles, only for Kindrack to find them and threaten them, telling the party that he knows everything about them and what they have done. He then calls for security to escort them and Mark out the building, and that if he sees them again he'll kill them. 

The party regroups, Freya and Mark come up with a operation plan. Before the operation, the party meets with Daliah in soul form, and shares more about her personal life and the full truth, in a heart to heart moment. The party then goes to a implant doctor, where Mark tells his story, and gives them a device to disable Daliah. Tartarus asks which side they are on, and gives his perspective. 
After, the party then executes the plan. They took control of the twins car and drove it to Mark and Freya's location, to where Mark and Freya killed them. Freya and Jaloop then posed as the twins, with the rest of the party posing as secuirty. The party make it to the meeting, and meet with Kindrack and Daliah. The party then makes their way down to the Boxed in Orb, and extracts it, siding with Daliah and not using Mark's device. The party makes a run for it and Daliah disables security. Freya kills Kindrack and takes out the guards, but is mad and tells Mark they got the slip. The party then runs away with Daliah, making out of the Metropolis alive. Daliah then says she'll contact them when she is ready for the next step of her plan to get them a cure. 

Sometime later, Daliah instructs the party on next steps. She needed to get to the Astral Port, a place where she can get a trip to the moon which she booked. The party makes their way back to Sigil, and see Daliah in person. Daliah is on her deathbed and slowly dying. The party then makes it through the airport and helps Daliah get in. They then find out that Titania, Mark and the Seelie Court are at the airport, looking for Daliah. Titania commands her men to capture her by any means necessary. Once the party make to the terminal, they exist a lift only to see conflict break out between Peacekeepers and the Seelie court, breaking a cease fire treaty made years ago. The party then rushes through the battle, and make it to the transit center that will have a train to take Daliah to her travel pod. The party is ambushed by Seelie Court members, Daliah continues to die, but uses her powerful magic to decimate the enemy forces. Monkey then takes her to the train when it arrives, setting off for her escape.

In the train however, Daliah left one part of the truth out. The orb and cure will only work for one person. But, she gives her the Flower of Soul's Persona that was sealed within her by cutting her own flesh out. Tartarus then makes a few comments, and gives Monkey a single shot Firearm imbued with Logic Slash. Monkey then takes Daliah to the bridge leading to the pod, only for Mark to intercept. The two have a western shoot off, with Monkey killing mark. He then kills Daliah after, taking the orb for himself. 

The party after the events, then make it back to the Sanctuary/Citadel and get ready for Azriel's ressurection.

###### Omnipresent War Arc
The party begins Azriel's resurrection, only Queen Titania to show up and resurrect her Black Ops squadron. The party is outmatched, and frantically attempts to hold off Titania and her squadron until the process is done. In a close fight between the two (including the death of Aeris Arendor and Monty), the party was able to fully complete the resurrection of Azriel Darkstar. After the fact, Titania and Azriel squared off, with the party taking care of the rest of the forces. Upon defeating Titania, Azriel was able to discern that they were being mind controlled by some external force, and told the party to make their way to Sigil while he takes care of a few things. 
It is at this point in time when the Raven Queen comes for Azriel's soul. Otto then kills Azriel, completing his contract but losing his powers. Azriel then appears as a Bat from ashes and from Olivia's cleavage emerges. He then makes Otto his Warlock, granting him his powers back, and a protector of Olvia and his family. 

Upon returning to Sigil, Monty along with the group were deemed the S-Rank title, and were instructed to visit Jackson Felwinter's office as a official rite of passage to becoming S-Rank. Upon making it to his office, Jackson congratulates them and assigns them to be his own personal squadron, and says he can provide anything they need up front. The party gives the orb they stole from Daliah in order to cure Monty of his disease, and Olivia Birdsong rushes into the office, having figured out the truth. Jackson then blitzes and impales her, revealing he is actually [[Arangol]]. Arangol offers them a seat and tea, explains that he was the one who created the Peacekeeper force, and that Jackson has been gone a long time. He reveals the truth of the past from Cour 2 of the Felwinter Saga, and shows the party that Jackson is trapped within a demon realm, and that his wife, [[Aria Hassan]] has been missing. As Monty and the party race to their car along with Emily, Yasu-2, and a dying Olivia. However they are not let off the handle easy, as enemy vehicles and forces begin chasing them as well. Upon making it to the portal, Gabriella Alucard, Gerald, Anna Efrideet, Freya, and Nathan Murgul arrive to the scene, and buy them time to open it and escape. The defense was a success, and the party jumps through the portal. 

The party is split and fell into the first of the nine layers of the hells. Monty is separated and with Jaloop and Emily, upon exploring the hells on foot, they hear fighting in the distance. As they move over the ridge they find Ludwig fighting a group of Abyssal demons. Joining his aid, they are able to help him dispatch the threat. Ludwig is pissed, and asks as to why they are in the hells. Monty and Jaloop brief him on everything, and their findings. Ludwig suspected as such, but didn't have the evidence to prove it. The three then take the car down the river styx, and make it to the ninth layer of hell, more specifically, Asmodeus's gates to his castle. The three are let in. The three are then out in front, and find the rest of the party. Then out of no where Aeris, Akanos and Aria appear. After a brief battle with Asmodeus, Aria and Asmodeus agree to disagree, and the party goes out to defeat Degalith, who was revealed to be Aeris's cousin. The party went over the plan, which was for half to distract Degalith, and the other to invade his lair to free Jackson Felwinter, who had been trapped there for centuries. Upon making it to the lair, the party was tricked, and faced the true Degalith, defeating him. Upon saving Jackson and escaping the Abyss, the party reunites with the other group. The two groups then set out to take back Sigil 

The party arrives as Sigil, where half fight of peacekeeper forces, and the other half (the PCs) fought Arangol. Upon defeating Arangol, the party was warned of something way bigger. 
After the fight, Monty went on a long self journey regarding the making of his own Runic language, which Azriel showed him before he left for his journey. He constructed a Warforged similar to how Jotor did with Yasu-2, and became overall a better Runekeeper. One day, the hostile Runekeeper force invaded Sigil. Monty, shocked, sensed a disturbance and other Runekeepers, and went to Sigil to investigate. Peacekeepers were attempting to save the city, and Jackson Felwinter and Azriel squared off against [[The Infinite Reader]]. After winning their respective battles, the party ventured to only find Jackson and Azriel brutally wounded and in a coma. The Infinite Reader then sought to team up with Arangol, who was trapped in a special hedron. As Arangol turns him down, he puts the Infinite reader under Narrative Change, manipulating the time he had to invade Sigil, and as the Infinite Reader returns, striking a deal to Monty, who then takes it and joins his Runekeeper people. Monty and the Infinite Reader then dissipate and returns to his parallel dimension. Distraught, Jaloop rushes back to his orphanage, to find half the people dead, and all of it burned down. He then mobilizes the Seelie court to help, and other Bards from other college to partake in the fight versus [[The Istoria Rytsarey]] on Sigil. 
Monty was then inducted to the Istoria Rytsarey through ceremony and was named the Fable of Fortune, allowing him to manipulate Fortune and Misfortune of anything he pleases. Monty however, had different motives. Azriel gave him one of his ancestors notebook. In summary, it read that he wished for Runekeepers to work cooperatively with society one day, and that not all of them were killed when they were purged by Jackson after Azriels fall to evil and defeat. It said that the Istoria Rytsarey and the Infinite Reader must be stopped. With this in mind, Monty made a plan to betray them, splitting himself into M and Y, Fortune and Misfortune, and sent one of them to the party.  
Upon fighting through puzzles and significant enemies, the party makes it right before the lair. The party is then split, and each member faces off against one of the Elites. The two halves of Monty met Alex. Monty staged Alex's death, attempting to fool the fable of reversal, and the two went towards the final battle in the Throne room
The party and Monty, who revealed that his whole plan was to double cross the Infinite Reader, make it to the throne room and face off against him. As the party is about to die, Arangol uses his Narrative Change, turning the tide, and Jackson and Azriel return. 
While this is happening, Monty was taken to a different place by the Fable of Reversal. Upon extended fighting, the Fable of Reversal deemed Monty strong enough, and revealed that he planned to take down the Infinite Reader as well. Sacrificing himself, he gave Monty a silver bullet that would disable the Infinite Reader's powers for 1-6 seconds. Monty then returns to the battle. Upon a hard fight, Monty shooting a Anti-Runekeeper bullet, Jackson; Azriel; and Arangol using their respective final blows, and Akanos's final hit, The Infinite Reader dissipates. A door shows in reality, and the party enters through to meet [[The Creator]]. As Monty and Jaloop fill in The Infinite Reader and the [[The Omnipotent One]]'s respective duties (Monty raced Azriel, giving Azriel a new chance at life and essentially sacrificing himself), the party returns to Sigil, and a celebration is held

###### Epilogue
Monty surprisingly attended Azriel and Olivia's wedding, an exception made by the Creator. However, upon becoming the The Infinite, Monty had and continues a strange fate. Upon taking the Infinite Readers position, he became the Infinite Reader himself in all timelines, meaning that he now lives in a endless loop of reading the same story over and over again, while existing in the story at the same time, putting him in an infinite loop of existence that he cannot break. But, because of this, fantasy is allowed to progress forwards and continue to exist, and it has slightly more free will and autonomy. 

###### Recruitment
Monty joined the peacekeeper force willingly and passed the trials along with 7 others. He is in a team with [[Otto]], [[Chuck]], [[Jaloop]], [[Akanos]], [[Monkey (Alex)]], [[Gorlock (Rhulk)]], [[Aeris Arendor]], and [[Equilibrium]]. He is currently a B-Rank peacekeeper.
