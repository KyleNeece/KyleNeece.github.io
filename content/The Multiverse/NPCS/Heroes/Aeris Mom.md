##### Brief Summary 
Aeris's mom is a Fey Wanderer High Elf Ranger and mother of [[Aeris Arendor]]
##### Appearance 

##### Personality 
Aeris's mom is a kind and wise soul, she loved the natural world and relaxed and explored nature all the time. She also was one of her village's hunters, hunting and providing food. When her village faced impending doom, she saved Aeris's father, and used her hunting and martial skills to help defeat the Eldrazi force. She then married Aeris's dad after, and the two live a happy life as higher status nobles.
##### Brief History/Timeline   
###### Origins
Aeris's mom grew up on Zendikar, and participated in the war as one of the elite forces vs the eldrazi and their forces. Her skill with a bow and druidic magic was one of the reasons why she was able to be such an effective combatant. After the war and having Aeris, she taught him about nature while [[Aeris Dad]] taught Aeris about the stars.
