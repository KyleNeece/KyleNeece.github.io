##### Brief Summary
 Shek Kai Yun is a Samurai Fighter originating from Kara-Tur 

Shek Kai Yun grew up on Kara-Tur as a both a Artist and Martial Arts Teacher (Specializing in Iaido), showing his mastery in Bun and Bu. Through understanding of Zen Buddhism, he is able to deeply empathize with others and understand others. He can do this from a Emotional, Philosophical, and Combat perspective. He tires as hard as he can to engage in non-violent ways and in peaceful manner. He earned his spot at the hotel simply from wandering there and understanding himself. He is always silent and communicates through emotion and gesture

###### Campaign 2
Shek Kai Yun upon arrival to the hotel instantly began painting beautiful works of art, and has not brushed up on his Iaido skills for a long time. Once he was able to tag along with the party to King [[Darrington Bant]]'s audience, he wanted to help the party on their journeys, and began practicing Iaido again in order to help them survive the harsh world. He seeks the Sword of History to learn absolute understanding of Zen Buddhism
