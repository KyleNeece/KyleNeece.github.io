##### Brief Summary
Rosa was the family puppet of the Felwinter family with a happy, sincere and carefree attitude. She was friends with [[Carson Collodi]] while he was still a human originally. She was found waiting at the Sigil Zoo for him. In her last moments of being around true friends at the Sigil friendship tree, she gifted a drawing of her, Carson, [[Ivy Felwinter]], and Rowan (later [[Rowan, Monarch of Puppets]]), [[Giuseppe Geppetto]], and Geppetto's wife. The writing on the back says 'our beloved son' and 'May your every day be as happy as this one' in two sentences with two different sets of handwriting.
