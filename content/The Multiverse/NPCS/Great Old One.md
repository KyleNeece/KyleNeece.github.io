##### Brief Summary 
A Great Old One is an Outer God who's powers rival or even triumph that of a normal pantheon gods. 
###### Brief Summary
Great Old Ones exist outside the traditional cosmology of the multiverse, they inhabit an unnamed place beyond the Far Realm, and are the closest to the Source Wall, which is the wall that separates fiction and reality
