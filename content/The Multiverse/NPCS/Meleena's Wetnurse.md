##### Brief Summary 
Meleena's Wetnurse is a manifestation made by the baby Great Old One Meleena, who is a human great old one hybrid that lacks a physical form and only has a conscience. It originates from The [[City of Evernight]]

##### Brief History/Timeline   
Since Meleena's death, Meleena wills the Wetnurse into existence in order to protect itself from [[Sai Mona]] and other threats. Meleena is the source of the nightmare of Sai Mona, and only when it is killed did the hunt temporarily end, and Gerald is able to free any hunters who partook in the yearly hunts.
