##### Brief Summary
Geneviève Forge was apart of the Forge clan. She originally fought [[Ivy Felwinter]] and lost in order to claim the bounty placed on her, but Ivy spared her, showing an act of kindness. However, the Occultists captured her and a Forge factory, experimented on her, and caused her to become maddened.
