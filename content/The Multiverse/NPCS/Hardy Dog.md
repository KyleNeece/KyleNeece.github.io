##### Brief Summary
Hardy Dog (HD) is the attack dog who belonged to [[Joshua Price]] and the Fire Opal Devils. Hardy Dog was found on a mission in the hells within the city of Elturel as an orphaned and sad puppy missing an eye. His was found by Joshua Price later on, and taken to the Fire Opal Devils base for her new temporary home. Shortly after, Fwip defected and Hardy Dog followed him as Joshua Price disappeared after defecting.
