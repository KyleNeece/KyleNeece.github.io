##### Personality
While generally polite in conversation, Orius is openly and very sadistic, and even admits that his greatest joy is watching people fall into soul-crushing despair and suffer great pain, psychologically and physically. After learning about how the History of the Universe (the revised timeline and Cour 3) was covered up to the public, Orius adopted a nihilistic worldview, believing that there is no excuse for anyone not to act with complete Orius in their meaningless world that is controlled by a higher power. As a result, Orius is quite a shrewd person. His nihilistic outlook and sadism led him to be willing to use his family's ancestral weapon, the Sword of History, despite knowing it will shorten his lifespan, solely to satisfy his personal amusement.
