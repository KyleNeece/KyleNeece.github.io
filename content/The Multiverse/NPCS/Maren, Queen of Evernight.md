##### Brief History/Timeline   
The Queen of Evernight was the ruler of the City of Evernight. However, when Dox Vulethix discovered Evernight, he impregnated Maren, having her birth children [[Reemus, the Inscrutable Spider]], [[Shadow of Evernight]], and Meleena ([[Meleena's Wetnurse]]). Because of this unwanted pregnancy, the queen spiraled into disappear and ultimately killed herself, and her soul wanders the city. She along with the rest of the City of Evernight, became trapped within [[Sai Mona]]'s dream.
