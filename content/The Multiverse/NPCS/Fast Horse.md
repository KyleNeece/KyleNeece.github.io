##### Brief Summary
Fast Horse (FH) is a skeleton pegasus steed ridden by [[Joshua Price]] and originally owned by [[Helter Skelter]]. Joshua would ride it into missions sometimes, and after his defection, the horse stayed with Helter Skelter.
