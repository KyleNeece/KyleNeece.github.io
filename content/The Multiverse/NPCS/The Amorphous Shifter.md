##### Brief Summary 
The Amorphous Shifter is a [[Great Old One]] Outer God, who was originally an Archdruid of the Star Circle
##### Appearance
![[The Amorphous Shifter.png]]
##### Brief History/Timeline   
The Amorphous shifter was once a fabled Archdruid, a powerful mortal who could take shape of almost anything in reality. He ascended to a higher plane of existence upon unlocking the true secrets to the stars, space, snd the abyss. 
His true form cannot be comprehended by any mortal and even some gods. The shifter had been waiting for a ambitious patron, who's fluidity and moral ambiguity is constantly shifting and changing, but at the same time is a wise individual. Because of this, he picked [[Aeris Arendor]] as his incarnation.
