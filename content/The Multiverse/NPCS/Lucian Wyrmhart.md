##### Origins
> [!Warning]- Spoilers Here DO NOT CLICK
When Jackson Felwinter went to investigate the Dryden and Efrideet murders, he was ambushed and subdued by Orius and his court. They sealed him within the far realm and stripped him of his fable. In order for him to not refind it within the astral sea, they imbued it within a normal cat, giving the cat human like qualities and a strong dosage of power. The cat was given the name Lucian Wyrmhart, the old cover name for Jackson Felwinter after he revised the timeline just in case anyone became suspicious of his identity on the nigh impossible chance they retained their memories from the universe reset. The only way for Jackson to have his fable returned to him would be for Lucian to die in a blaze of glory.
