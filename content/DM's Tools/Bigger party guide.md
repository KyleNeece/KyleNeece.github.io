- Encounters with minions in combat 
- Try to get everyone involved at least once during the session 
-  Skip Turns if not ready 
- Encounter key 
	- Easy and medium encounter - joke
	- Hard - Easy 
	- Deadly - Medium 
	- Deadly x 2 - Hard
	- Deadly 3x - Deadly
- Add more interesting actions to monsters
- Lots of HP, variable HP ranges
- Abilities and environments that grant the enemy mobility and restrict players mobility
- Allies whose abilities have synergy with BBEG abilities
- Immunities and resistances that most be discovered
- Increase enemy damage output slightly
- Look for team weaknesses
- Variable Spell save DC
- "Quantum Zombies or Rakghouls"
- Small Narrative reasons to empower enemies
