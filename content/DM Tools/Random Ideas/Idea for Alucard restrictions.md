**Alucard II restriction 0** 

Level Restirction Levels
- Level 3: Loses most powers and is a typical Vampire Spawn 
- Level 2: Current Level, can't use the special abilities of familiars but can use them to regenerate.
- Level 1: Gives access to 
	- Allows access to a limited amount of familiars, which are separate entities who's souls are bound to the vampire that consumed them, also increases overall power.
	- Appearance: Her body becomes enveloped in dark black energy (except the head) like a second skin and millions of eyes open and cover the black energy
- Level 0 
	- Allows full access to all familiars and souls absorbed 
	- Includes Millions of Familiars 
	- Gains the ability to flood numerous cities with thousands of _familiars_ and _blood_; because of this, the Level is also referred to as the river of blood
	- But loses her immortality 
		- Appearance: 
			- Assumes her original form, Valerie the Impaler/Draculina
			- Full Plate Armor, hair grows out, gains access to her longsword
			- Can change her appearance and equipment at will
