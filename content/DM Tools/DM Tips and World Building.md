- Fake choices. Players can see through them. 
- No rolls. Failure should create consequences, not direct punishments. 
- Ignoring character moments. Even if they're new, let them try to connect on an emotional level. 
- Rushed choices. Ask what players are thinking, and don't force action until such actions prove necessary.
- Useless backstory. Let players use their backstory to succeed or fail. 
- Imposed backstory. Don't tell players what their character is about - ask for details and integrate them live, or off camera.
- Lack of impact. Make sure players can change your plot threads, even dramatically
