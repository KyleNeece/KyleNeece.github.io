0.5 Side quest
Session Plan: "The Goblin's Wish"

Objective:
The party must neutralize the chaotic effects of a goblin’s wish that has warped the forest. They must either outwit the goblin or force him to undo the wish, restoring the forest to its natural state.

Part 1: The Encounter with the Goblin (30 minutes)  

1. Introduction (5 minutes):
   - Description: The session begins with the party traveling through a dense, dark forest. The atmosphere is eerie, with strange noises echoing through the trees.
   - Hook: They hear a booming voice: "Your Wish has been granted!" The voice is followed by maniacal laughter.
   - Skill Checks: Allow players to make Perception (DC 13) or Survival (DC 12) checks to track the source of the sound.

2. The Clearing (10 minutes):
   - Description: The party enters a clearing shrouded in red mist. In the center, they see Grig the Greedy, a goblin holding a brass lamp, surrounded by twisted, hostile flora.
   - Roleplay: Grig is delighted with his newfound power, though he clearly doesn’t understand the full consequences. He taunts the party, threatening them with his "unmatched power."

3. Initial Combat/Challenge (15 minutes):
   - Encounter: The twisted trees around the clearing come to life as Grig commands them to attack. Use statistics for animated trees or similar creatures. Grig stays at a distance, using his creatures to attack the party.
   - Alternative: If the party tries to talk to Grig, allow a Persuasion or Deception check (DC 15) to convince him to share more about his wish and the lamp.


Part 2: Exploring the Twisted Forest (1 hour)

4. Discovering the Effects of the Wish (20 minutes):
   - Description: After the initial encounter, Grig flees deeper into the forest. The party must pursue him while navigating the warped forest. The trees seem to whisper, and normal animals have mutated into grotesque creatures.
   - Encounters: Along the way, the party faces several encounters:
   - Mutant Beasts: Use stats for enhanced versions of common forest creatures (e.g., giant boars, dire wolves).
   - Living Vines: Areas where the forest itself attacks with animated vines and roots.
   - Hazardous Terrain: Poisonous plants, quicksand, or thick, blinding fog.
   - Skill Challenge: The party can use Nature, Survival, or Arcana checks (DC 14) to navigate safely. Failure results in minor environmental damage or leading into an ambush.


5. Clues and Lore (20 minutes):
   - Description: The party comes across remnants of other adventurers or inhabitants affected by the wish. These could be:
   - A ruined camp: Where the adventurers were overwhelmed by the forest.
   - An ancient stone tablet: That speaks of the lamp’s true nature and hints at reversing its effects.
   - Roleplay: The party may find a survivor who witnessed Grig’s wish and offers crucial information, such as the fact that the wish can be undone by the lamp’s holder.

1. Pursuing Grig (20 minutes):
   - Description: The party eventually tracks Grig to a secluded part of the forest, where the effects of the wish are strongest. Here, the trees are colossal, and the ground trembles with power.
   - Skill Checks: Stealth (DC 15) to approach undetected, or Intimidation (DC 14) to force Grig to engage with them.

Part 3: The Final Confrontation (45 minutes)
1. Confronting Grig (15 minutes):
   - Description: The party finds Grig in the center of a magical grove, where he is trying (and failing) to control his powers. He is desperate and might even beg the party for help.
   - Roleplay: Grig is paranoid, seeing threats everywhere. He might offer to share his power or use the wish to "help" the party, but his lack of control makes him a dangerous ally.

2. The Final Battle or Diplomacy (30 minutes):
   - Combat Option: If the party chooses to fight, Grig will unleash the full power of the wish, summoning powerful monsters and animating the forest against the party. Use stats for creatures like treants or large elementals.
   - Diplomatic Option: The party can convince Grig to undo the wish (Persuasion, Deception, or Intimidation, DC 16). They can also trick him into making another wish that nullifies the first.
   - Resolution: If the wish is undone, the forest slowly returns to normal. Grig may be left powerless, offering the lamp to the party in exchange for his life, or he could be defeated and the lamp claimed as loot.

Part 4: Epilogue and Hook for Next Session (15 minutes)
1. The Forest Restored (5 minutes):
   - Description: As the forest heals, the red mist dissipates The party is left with the lamp.
2. The Courier and the Haunted Mansion (10 minutes):
   - Description As the party exits the forest, they are approached by a breathless courier who delivers a letter. The letter informs them they have inherited a mansion from a landlord, who offered them a stay as he watched them chase Grig and undo the wish, where he returns the favor by having them rest at the mansion as a part of his treat. The party has to live in it for a few days
   - Ending Hook: The session ends with the ominous promise of a haunted mansion awaiting the party, setting the stage for the next adventure.

Session Notes:
- Pacing: Adjust the time spent on each section based on party interest and engagement.
- Rewards: Consider granting the party minor magical items or boons from the restored forest.
- Difficulty: Scale encounters based on party level and composition.

This session is designed to be flexible, allowing for both combat and roleplay, with a strong focus on environmental challenges and puzzle-solving. The ending hook transitions smoothly into the next adventure, keeping the party engaged and eager for more.
