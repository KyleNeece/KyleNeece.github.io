**For UVM 2024-2025 Campaign**
For those of you who missed last session or need a reminder on the new rules for act 2, here they are.
- Out of combat initiative 
	- Helps with people who may not participate as much due to spotlight things
- Roll a hit die health encounters 
	- Sometimes I'll ask you to roll your hit die for the day. The number you roll = the number of hits you can take for the day. Damage calculations work differently, but I can help you with those in session 

- Stealth System 
	- Similar to stealth video games, there will be a detection value if a character sees something suspicious. If the meter hits the max, combat will initiate if the character is hostile 
- Timer system (Min/Hour/Days)
	- Between quests you will have time to explore the Thieves Metropolis. You can do side quests, mercenary jobs, and other activities. This is the day system. You'll be assigned a date to finish a main quest, you can move as fast or slow to that as you want 
	- Sometimes you'll get a progress clock, which means that you'll have to complete an objective or mission in a certain amount of time or else you fail
- Influence system 
	- Your interactions with NPCS within the world (more so in Thieves Metropolis), will either increase or decrease influence value. Depending on the value of the NPC, you'll get info, rewards, and other outcomes depending on your influence with them
